## Pattern Discovery: Consultant Wedge Strategy

**Problem: Pharma Sales Cycles**

Pharma has notorious 12-24 month sales cycles that kill "Speed to Revenue" scores:
- Quality teams insulated from vendors
- Regulatory scrutiny on all tooling
- GxP validation requirements
- Multi-stakeholder approval processes

**Solution: Consultant Channel**

Audit Response AI succeeds by targeting quality consultants:
- Consultants hired **immediately** post-audit (crisis mode)
- Consultants serve **100+ clients** over their career
- Consultants need **leverage** (tool amplifies their value)
- Consultants **recommend tools** (trusted advisor status)

**The Wedge Economics:**
| Direct Sales | Consultant Wedge |
|--------------|------------------|
| 18-24 month cycle | Immediate access (consultant already engaged) |
| 1 customer per effort | 100+ customers per consultant relationship |
| Cold outreach | Warm introduction (crisis mode) |
