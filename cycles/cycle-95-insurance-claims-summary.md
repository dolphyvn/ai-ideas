## Pattern Discovery: Underserved Mid-Market Strategy

**Incumbent Avoidance in Insurance:**

| Incumbent Focus | Market Served | Underserved Opportunity | Winner |
|-----------------|---------------|----------------------|--------|
| **Guidewire, Snapscreen, Claimatic** | Large carriers | Independent adjusters (500K+) | Adjuster Workflow (8.4) |
| **Carrier SIU tools** | Carrier SIU teams | Independent firms | Bad Faith Prevention (8.1) |
| **Carrier compliance systems** | Enterprise compliance | Mid-market firms | Claims Compliance (7.6) |

**Insight:** Incumbents focus on:
- **Enterprise customers** ($100K+ ACV, 12-24 month sales cycles)
- **Integrated platforms** (complex, expensive)
- **Carrier-first products** (built for insurer workflows)

**Underserved opportunity:**
- **Mid-market/individuals** ($1K-10K ACV, faster sales)
- **Standalone tools** (point solutions, not platforms)
- **Adjuster/firm-first** (independent businesses, not carriers)

**Success factors for underserved mid-market:**
1. **Point solution focus** (not "we replace Guidewire")
2. **Self-service onboarding** (no enterprise implementation)
3. **Transparent pricing** (published prices, not "contact us")
4. **Fast value delivery** (8-week MVP, instant value)
5. **Community-driven distribution** (forums, word-of-mouth)
