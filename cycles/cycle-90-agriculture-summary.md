## Pattern Discovery: Intermediary Multiplier Effect

**Agriculture Distribution Challenge:**

- 2M+ US farms (fragmented, hard to reach individually)
- Relationship-driven culture (trust required)
- Thin margins (3-5%) = price sensitivity

**Solution: Consultant/Advisor Wedge**

| Intermediary | Serves | Winner | Multiplier |
|--------------|--------|--------|------------|
| Farm Labor Contractors | 5-20 farms | H-2A Comply AI | 10-20x reach |
| Pest Control Advisors | 20-50 farms | Pesticide Comply AI | 20-50x reach |
| Crop Consultants | 10-30 farms | Nutrient Mgmt (6.7) | - |

**Insight:** Instead of selling to 2,000 farms directly, sell to 100 consultants who collectively serve 5,000 farms. This bypasses:
- Trust barrier (consultants already trusted)
- Fragmented customer acquisition
- Price sensitivity (consultants absorb cost, pass to farms)
