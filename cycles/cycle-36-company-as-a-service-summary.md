## Cycle 36 Insights

### Why All Failed:

**The Service Business Paradox**: Company-as-a-service ideas are SERVICE BUSINESSES first, software second. The core value is HUMAN EXPERTISE (CFO, CTO, CMO, HR, Legal), not the software. Software is just a tool for specialists to deliver services better.

**Service Business Complexity**:
- Recruiting specialized talent (CFOs, CTOs, CMOs)
- Quality control (service delivery consistency)
- Training/onboarding
- This makes 16-20 week MVPs the minimum

**Valid Businesses, Not AI-Native Software**: All 5 ideas are VALID business opportunities (fractional services are established markets). But they're service businesses that could use software, not AI-native software platforms.

### Key Discovery:

**8-Week MVP Filter = AI-Native Software Filter**: The AI Venture Grid Lab is designed to find AI-NATIVE SOFTWARE opportunities that can be validated in 8 weeks. Company-as-a-service = SERVICE BUSINESSES first, software second.
