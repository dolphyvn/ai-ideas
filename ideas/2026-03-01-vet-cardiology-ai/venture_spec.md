# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**VetCardiologyAI - Veterinary Cardiology Platform**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered platform for veterinary cardiologists that automates echocardiogram interpretation, heart disease diagnosis, cardiac medication management, and pet owner communication.

## Problem:
- **2,000+ veterinary cardiologists** in US
- **Heart disease = common**: 10% of dogs develop heart disease (mitral valve disease, DCM)
- **Echocardiogram cost**: $300-$800 per echo (expensive for pet owners)
- **Echo interpretation**: Specialist interpretation required (few veterinary cardiologists)
- **Heart medications**: Pimobendan, furosemide, enalapril = lifelong treatment
- **Pet owner anxiety**: Heart disease diagnosis = emotional, life-altering

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** Veterinary Cardiologists
- Board-certified veterinary cardiologists
- General practice vets (echo interpretation service)
- Emergency vet hospitals (cardiology consults)
- Veterinary cardiology mobile services

**Secondary:** Pet owners, pet insurance companies, general practice vets

## Why Now:
1. **Pet longevity**: Pets living longer = more age-related heart disease
2. **Echo availability**: Veterinary echo machines more common (cheaper, portable)
3. **Cardiology drugs**: Pimobendan, newer heart meds = improved outcomes
4. **Pet insurance**: Pet insurance growth = more cardiac workups covered
5. **Mobile cardiology**: Vet cardiology mobile services growing

## AI Advantage:
- **Echo interpretation AI**: Computer vision (ultrasound imaging) analyzes echocardiograms
- **Heart disease detection**: AI identifies mitral valve disease, DCM, pericardial effusion
- **Severity scoring**: AI calculates cardiac severity scores (prognosis)
- **Medication management**: AI tracks heart meds, drug interactions, dosing
- **GP echo service**: AI enables general practice vets to offer echo interpretation
- **Pet owner education**: AI generates heart disease education materials

## Viral Mechanism:
- **ACVIM conferences**: American College of Veterinary Internal Medicine (Cardiology)
- **Veterinary conferences**: VMX, NAVC
- **Pet owner social media**: Instagram, TikTok pet accounts = massive
- **Case studies**: "Diagnosed heart condition early using AI" = VIRAL
- **Pet insurance**: Partnerships with Trupanion, Nationwide, etc.

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Practice Subscription:**
- **Starter**: $1,000/month - 1-3 veterinarians, echo AI + cardiac meds
- **Practice**: $2,500/month - 4+ veterinarians, unlimited + GP echo service
- **Enterprise**: $6,000/month - Multi-location + white-label + API + pet portal

**Per-Echo add-on:**
- $25 per AI echo interpretation (beyond included tiers)

**Implementation:**
- $4,000 one-time (echo AI training, cardiology setup, training)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $150 (ACVIM, veterinary conferences)
- LTV: $54,000 (36-month retention - switching costs high)
- Gross Margin: 82%

## Moat:
1. **Veterinary cardiology expertise**: Board certification, echo interpretation, heart meds
2. **Echo AI model**: Ultrasound imaging AI trained on veterinary echocardiograms
3. **Heart disease complexity**: Mitral valve disease, DCM, pericardial effusion, congenital defects
4. **Medication protocols**: Pimobendan dosing, furosemide, enalapril, cardiology drug interactions
5. **GP echo network**: General practice vets = referral network

## MVP in 12 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-3:**
- Focus: Echo interpretation only
- ONE condition (mitral valve disease)
- One vet cardiologist pilot

**Weeks 4-6:**
- Build AI echo interpreter (ultrasound imaging - CNN)
- Mitral valve severity scorer
- Heart disease classifier

**Weeks 7-9:**
- Partner with 3 vet cardiologists + 5 GP vets
- Deploy pilot for echo interpretation
- Validate: AI accuracy vs. specialist interpretation

**Weeks 10-12:**
- Add 5 more conditions (DCM, pericardial effusion, congenital, HCM, arrhythmias)
- Medication management module
- Prepare ACVIM conference demo

**Validation Metrics:**
- 90%+ echo interpretation accuracy (vs. veterinary cardiologist)
- 50%+ faster echo interpretation
- 3+ specialists commit after pilot
- One heart disease detected early using AI

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: PyTorch (ultrasound imaging - CNN, ResNet), GPT-4 (medication management)
- **Integrations**: Veterinary PACS, ultrasound machines, EHR systems
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (US regions, secure)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $12K MRR (12 practices × $1K ARPU)
- **Year 2**: $60K MRR (60 practices + practice tier)
- **Year 3**: $180K MRR (180 practices + enterprise)

## Risk Factors:
1. **AI liability**: Wrong heart disease diagnosis = delayed treatment = liability
2. **Echo quality**: Variable echo quality (operator-dependent)
3. **Veterinary competition**: Veterinary cardiology = specialized but growing
4. **Pet owner cost**: Echo + cardiology consult = expensive ($500-$1,000)
5. **Heart disease stigma**: Some owners elect euthanasia (quality of life decision)

## Competitive Threat:
- **Vet cardiology services**: Mobile cardiology services (not AI-focused)
- **Veterinary ultrasound companies**: SonoPath, IDEXX (not AI interpretation)
- **General practice vets**: Some offer in-house echo (not specialist)

**Differentiation**: Only AI platform for veterinary cardiology with echo interpretation AI, heart disease detection, and GP echo service.
