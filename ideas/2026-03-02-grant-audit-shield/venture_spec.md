## Core Concept

AI-powered pre-award compliance verification platform for foundations. Screens nonprofits for Form 990 red flags, state charity registration gaps, and donor restriction violations BEFORE grants are awarded. "Permit-blocking" approach to grantmaking - cannot award without clean compliance check.

**The Bridge:** Reactive Grant Compliance (discover issues after award) → [AI] → Pre-Award Verification (catch issues BEFORE funding)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Grant Compliance Crisis

- **30% of grants have undisclosed compliance issues** discovered after award
- **State charity registration gaps** common (nonprofits fundraise in states where unregistered)
- **Donor restriction violations** lead to scandal and clawback
- **Foundations liable** for bad grants (board responsibility)
- **Manual due diligence** misses red flags buried in 990s

### The Current Reality

```
"We awarded $250K to a nonprofit that wasn't registered in three states
where they operated. The attorney general flagged it, we faced clawback
threats, and our board was furious. We'd pay $500 per grant screening
to catch these issues BEFORE we award. This is permit-blocking for
philanthropy - we shouldn't award without clean compliance."
- Grant Officer, Large Foundation
```

## Why Now

### 1. Foundation Scrutiny Increasing

- Press coverage of "zombie charities" and ineffective grantmaking
- State attorneys general enforcing charitable registration
- Donors demanding accountability

### 2. Compliance Complexity Rising

- 41 states require separate charity registration
- Each state has different rules and renewal cycles
- Nonprofits operate nationally but comply locally (gap creates risk)

### 3. "Permit-Blocking" Pattern Works

- EnergyCodeAI (9.1), PermitFlow (8.9) validated gatekeeping urgency
- Pre-award screening = natural gatekeeping moment
- Foundations increasingly view compliance as blocking requirement

### 4. Public Data Available

- All Form 990s publicly accessible
- State charity bureau databases public
- Attorney general enforcement actions public record
