## Core Concept

AI-powered restaurant wage law compliance platform that continuously monitors labor practices against federal/state/municipal wage laws, proactively identifies violation risks (misclassification, overtime calculation errors, tip credit violations), generates corrective action plans, and produces audit-ready defense documentation.

**The Bridge:** Reactive Wage Claims (lawsuits, $50K-500K settlements) → [AI] → Proactive Compliance Monitoring (risk detection, audit defense)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Wage Claim Crisis

- **$50K-500K average settlement** for wage violation claims
- **DOL enforcement up 300%** in past 5 years
- **50 states + 20,000 municipalities** with varying wage laws
- **Tip credit violations** rampant and costly
- **Class actions exploding** - single violation = years of liability

### The Current Reality

```
"We got hit with a $200K wage claim for overtime calculation errors
we didn't even know we were making. Turned out our tip credit
accounting had been wrong for 3 years. By the time we settled,
we'd lost our entire year's profit. If we'd had AI that actually
flagged this risk before the DOL showed up, we'd have paid $50K
annually in a heartbeat."

```
