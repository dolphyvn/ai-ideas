### LTV Assumptions

- **Average Revenue Per Account (ARPA):** $165/month
- **Gross Margin:** 85% (software), 40% (services)
- **Churn:** 15% annual (industry standard for vertical SaaS)
- **Expansion Revenue:** 5% annually
- **Lifetime:** ~5.5 years

**LTV Calculation:**
```
LTV = (ARPA × Gross Margin × (1 + Expansion)) / Churn
LTV = ($165 × 12 × 0.85 × 1.05) / 0.15
LTV = $11,700
```

**LTV:CAC Ratio:** 11,700 / 145 = **80:1** (excellent)

## Pricing Strategy

### Anchor Pricing

**Competitive Positioning:**
- Leanpath: $5,000+ annually
- Winnow: £3,000+ annually
- Our price: $1,188-$5,988 annually

**Positioning Statement:**
> "Enterprise-grade compliance at 20% of the cost"

### Pricing Psychology

1. **Good-Better-Best:**
   - $99 (anchor for "under $100")
   - $199 (pushes to middle option)
   - $299 (aspirational for growing chains)

2. **Monthly Pricing:**
   - Reduces upfront commitment anxiety
   - Aligns with monthly operational expenses

3. **Location-Based:**
   - Natural expansion lever
   - Fair pricing (more locations = more value)

### Dynamic Pricing Opportunities

1. **City-Based:**
   - Premium for high-enforcement cities (SF, LA)
   - Discount for low-enforcement areas

2. **Tier-Based:**
   - Premium for Tier 1 businesses (higher fine risk)
   - Discount for Tier 3-4 (lower compliance burden)

3. **Seasonal:**
   - Peak season pricing for seasonal businesses
   - Annual payment discount (20% off)

## Churn Reduction

### Proactive Churn Prevention

1. **Risk Detection:**
   - Declining usage (no waste entries in 30 days)
   - Payment failures
   - Support ticket spike

2. **Intervention:**
   - CSM outreach for at-risk accounts
   - Win-back offers (month free, discount)
   - Feature training webinars

3. **Success Metrics:**
   - Time to first value (< 7 days)
   - Weekly active usage
   - Compliance achieved

### Switching Costs

1. **Data Migration Cost:**
   - Historical compliance records
   - Exemption filings
   - Custom report configurations

2. **Process Integration:**
   - Staff trained on mobile app
   - Hauler API connections
   - Municipal paperwork

3. **Relationship Investment:**
   - Dedicated CSM
   - Compliance advisory
   - Ongoing exemption renewals

**Target Churn:**
- Y1: 20% (early stage volatility)
- Y2: 18%
- Y3: 15%
- Y4+: 12% (industry best practice)
