## Pricing Strategy

### Tier Structure

| Tier | Price | Target Customer | VC Limit | Features |
|------|-------|-----------------|----------|----------|
| **Starter** | $499/mo | Solo founders, pre-seed | 100/month | Basic research, email sequences, templates |
| **Pro** | $1,249/mo | Seed stage, serious | 500/month | Everything + CRM, A/B testing, analytics |
| **Enterprise** | $2,499/mo | Series A, teams | Unlimited | Everything + API access, white-label, support |
| **Done-for-you** | $5K one-time | Hands-off founders | - | Setup service, custom templates |

### Annual Billing Incentive

- **2 months free** for annual commitments
- **Effective discount:** ~17%
- **Annual Starter:** $4,998/year (vs $5,988 monthly)
- **Annual Pro:** $12,498/year (vs $14,988 monthly)

|---------|-----|----------|------------|
| Product-led/viral | $200 | 40% | $80 |

| Paid ads | $1,200 | 10% | $120 |
| Partnerships | $500 | 5% | $25 |
| **Blended** | - | **100%** | **$330 |

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Assumptions:**
- Average customer lifespan: 4 months (fundraising duration)
- But 30% re-purchase for next round (extension)
- 10% expand to higher tiers
- Net dollar retention: 120%

| Tier | Monthly Revenue | Avg Duration | LTV |
|------|----------------|--------------|-----|
| Starter | $499 | 4 months | $1,996 |
| Pro | $1,249 | 6 months | $7,494 |
| Enterprise | $2,499 | 12 months | $29,988 |
| **Weighted Avg** | - | - | **$6,000** |

### LTV:CAC Ratio

- **LTV:** $6,000
- **CAC:** $330
- **Ratio:** 18.2:1 (excellent)

### Payback Period

- **CAC:** $330
- **Monthly Revenue:** $700 (avg)
- **Payback:** < 1 month

## Revenue Recognition

### Booking vs Revenue

| Metric | Definition |
|--------|------------|
| **Bookings** | Contracts signed (total value) |
| **Revenue** | Revenue recognized (ratably) |
| **Deferred Revenue** | Prepaid annual contracts |
| **Billings** | Invoices paid |

### Accounting Treatment

- Monthly subscriptions: Recognize ratably
- Annual prepayments: Recognize over contract term
- Setup fees: Recognize upon delivery
- API usage: Recognize as consumed

### Key Assumptions

### Risks to Revenue Model

1. **Price sensitivity:** Founders may resist $500+ pricing
2. **Churn:** High natural churn due to finite fundraising
3. **Competition:** Free or cheaper alternatives emerge
4. **Deliverability:** Email issues reduce value perception
5. **Market timing:** Funding downturn reduces demand
