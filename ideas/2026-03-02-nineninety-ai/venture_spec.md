## Core Concept

AI-powered IRS Form 990 preparation and compliance platform that transforms raw financial data into filed 990 returns. The system uses semantic understanding of 990 schedules, cross-references prior year filings for anomaly detection, and auto-generates required narrative disclosures from organizational activity logs.

**The Bridge:** Manual 990 Preparation (spreadsheets, expensive CPAs) → [AI] → Automated Drafting (prior-year intelligence, red-flag detection)

---

## Problem Statement

### The 990 Compliance Burden

- **300,000+ nonprofits** required to file Form 990 annually
- **$2,000-15,000 per filing** for CPA preparation
- **Late penalties: $20/day** per organization
- **Audit triggers:** Errors on public 990s invite scrutiny
- **Narrative sections:** Complex requirements (Parts III, VI, IX)
