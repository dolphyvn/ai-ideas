# Validation Plan: NurseLC NLC Tracker

## Hypothesis
Staffing agencies will pay $500+/month for automated nurse license tracking because each prevented lapse saves $10,000+ in placement disruptions.

## Validation Method: Concierge MVP

### Week 1: Manual Service Test
**Action:**
- Identify 10 mid-sized travel nurse agencies (100-500 travelers each)
- Cold email with offer: "Free license audit - we'll find your expiring licenses this month"
- Manually scrape public nurse license databases for their travelers
- Deliver report: "12 of your 237 travelers have licenses expiring in 30 days"

**Success Metric:**
- 3/10 agencies reply interested
- 1 agency agrees to paid pilot

### Week 2-3: Landing Page + Waitlist
**Action:**
- Launch simple landing page (Vercel + Next.js)
- Value prop headline: "Stop Losing $10K Placements to Expired Licenses"
- CTA: "Get Free License Audit" + "Join Waitlist"
- Run LinkedIn ads targeting staffing agency owners
- Post in travel nurse Facebook groups (value-first, not spam)

**Success Metric:**
- 50 waitlist signups from agencies
- 20 waitlist signups from individual nurses
- 5 agencies request full audit

### Week 4: Pilot Program
**Action:**
- Onboard 1-2 agencies into manual concierge service
- Weekly email: "Here are your travelers expiring in next 30 days"
- Include: renewal requirements, direct links to state renewal portals
- Track: Did they act on alerts? Any lapses prevented?

**Success Metric:**
- Agency reports at least 1 prevented lapse
- Agency willing to pay $500+ for continued service
- Net Promoter Score > 8

## Ad Test Hypotheses

### Hypothesis 1: Fear-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "Your Travel Nurse License Expires in 27 Days. Do You Know Which Ones?"

**Body:** Last month, Agency X lost $47,000 in placements because 3 nurses arrived with expired licenses. We catch expirations 30 days in advance.

**CTA:** Get Free License Audit

### Hypothesis 2: Revenue-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "1 Expired License = $10,000 Lost Revenue"

**Body:** Automated multistate license tracking for travel nurse agencies. We flag expiring licenses 30 days before renewal. Stop losing placements.

**CTA:** See Your At-Risk Travelers

### Hypothesis 3: ROI-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "$500/month vs $47,000 in Lost Placements"

**Body:** Staffing agencies using NurseLC reduced license-related placement failures by 94%. Track all 50 states, NLC compliance, CNE requirements automatically.

**CTA:** Start Free Trial

## Success Criteria

**Minimum Viable Signal (Week 4):**
- 1 agency pays $500+ for pilot
- 10+ agencies on waitlist
- Clear evidence of prevented lapses

**Go/No-Go Decision (Week 4):**
- ✅ GO: 2+ paying agencies OR 50+ waitlist with strong intent
- ❌ NO-GO: 0 paying agencies AND <20 waitlist

## Pivot Options if Validation Fails

**Pivot 1: Direct-to-Nurse B2C**
- If agencies won't pay, test $15/month subscription with individual travel nurses
- Value prop: "Never lose a day's pay to license lapse again"

**Pivot 2: Compliance-as-a-Service**
- Full-service compliance: we handle renewals end-to-end
- Higher price point ($2,000-5,000/month), higher touch

**Pivot 3: Professional License Expansion**
- Expand beyond nurses to doctors, therapists, pharmacists
- Platform for all healthcare professional license tracking
