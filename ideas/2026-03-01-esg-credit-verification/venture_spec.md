# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**AI Environmental Credit Verification Platform**

## Core Concept:
Automated verification, validation, and certification of environmental credits (carbon, biodiversity, water) with regulatory-approved AI auditors. Handles the 2026 ESG disclosure wave through ISO-accredited AI verification.

## Problem:
- **2026 compliance deadlines**: Mandatory ESG disclosure starting April 2026
- **Verification bottleneck**: 18-24 month wait for human verifiers
- **Greenwashing crisis**: 40%+ of carbon credits may be worthless
- **Manual processes**: Spreadsheets and paper-based verification
- **Talent shortage**: 10M+ verifiers needed, only 100K available

## Target Vertical:
**Primary**: Environmental credit project developers
- Carbon offset projects (reforestation, renewable energy)
- Biodiversity credits (conservation, restoration)
- Water quality credits (wetland restoration)
- Corporate sustainability teams (buying credits)

**Secondary**: Verification bodies (white-label AI), regulators

## Why Now:
1. **Hard deadlines**: March 2026 EU Deforestation Regulation, April 2026 China ESG
2. **CBAM enforcement**: Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism active
3. **ISO 14064/14065**: AI-acceptable under updated standards
4. **Corporate urgency**: Non-compliance = market exclusion
5. **Regulatory acceptance**: ISO bodies approving AI-augmented verification

## AI Advantage:
- **Satellite analysis**: Automated biomass measurement (forestry)
- **Remote sensing**: IoT sensor data aggregation
- **Pattern detection**: Fraud detection in credit data
- **Compliance checking**: Real-time regulatory rule application
- **Report generation**: Audit-ready documentation

## Viral Mechanism:
- **Regulatory deadline**: Urgency drives word-of-mouth
- **Industry associations**: Verra, Gold Standard referrals
- **Corporate case studies**: "How we avoided $50M in compliance costs"
- **Verifier partnerships**: White-label to verification bodies

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Project Verification:**
- **Standard**: $5,000 per credit project - Up to 50K credits
- **Complex**: $15,000 per project - Biodiversity, multi-site
- **Enterprise**: $100K/year - Unlimited verifications for large developers

**White-Label:**
- Revenue share with verification bodies

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $2,000 (industry conferences, partnerships)
- LTV: $25,000 (avg 5 projects per developer)
- Gross Margin: 70%

## Moat:
1. **ISO accreditation**: 18-24 month process, significant barrier
2. **Regulatory relationships**: Verra, Gold Standard approvals
3. **Scientific data**: Proprietary biomass/biodiversity models
4. **Sensor network**: IoT data aggregation
5. **Fraud detection**: Credit validation database

## MVP in 7 Days:
**Days 1-2:**
- Focus: ONE credit type (carbon forestry)
- Manual satellite data ingestion
- Template verification report

**Days 3-4:**
- Open-source satellite APIs (Sentinel, Landsat)
- Biomass estimation model
- Regulatory checklist (ISO 14064)

**Days 5-7:**
- Partner with 1 project developer
- Verify 1 real project
- Get verification body to review AI output

**Validation Metric:**
- 1 verification body accepts AI-augmented report
- 80% time reduction vs. manual
- 1 developer commits to pilot

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **Satellite**: Sentinel Hub, Google Earth Engine
- **IoT**: AWS IoT Core (sensor aggregation)
- **AI**: Claude 4.6 (document analysis), Geospatial ML models
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (spatial), Qdrant (project data)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $50K MRR (10 projects/month × $5K)
- **Year 2**: $250K MRR (50 projects/month + white-label)
- **Year 3**: $750K MRR (150 projects + enterprise + regulatory)

## Risk Factors:
1. **Accreditation timeline**: ISO 14065 takes 18-24 months
2. **Regulatory rejection**: AI may not be accepted
3. **Scientific accuracy**: Wrong estimates = liability
4. **Greenwashing backlash**: If AI credits fail, market collapses
5. **Platform risk**: Google/Amazon may build this

## Competitive Threat:
- Sylvera (carbon credit rating, not verification)
- Pachama (forest monitoring, not ISO-accredited)
- Traditional verifiers (manual, slow)

**Differentiation**: ISO-accredited AI verification, not just monitoring
