# Revenue Model: RizzScore

## Core Value Proposition
Find better dates. $19/month to stop wasting time on bad conversations.

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier:**
- 3 message rewrites per day
- Basic profile feedback
- Community access (see others' success stories)
- Purpose: User acquisition and habit formation

**Pro Tier - $19/month:**
- Unlimited message rewrites
- Advanced profile analysis (bio + photo selection)
- Conversation review (what went wrong)
- Early access to new features
- Priority response time

**VIP Tier - $39/month:**
- Everything in Pro
- Real-time suggestions during conversations
- Photo ranking (which photos get most matches)
- Red flag detection (early warning signs)
- Personalized dating strategy

**Add-ons:**
- **Profile Overhaul** - $49 one-time
  - Complete bio rewrite
  - Photo selection and ranking
  - Prompt optimization for Hinge
- **Date Prep** - $9 per date
  - Conversation topics
  - Outfit advice
  - Venue suggestions

## Viral Loop Economics

**K-Factor Target: 1.2**

How users share:
1. **Before/after screenshots** - natural TikTok content
2. "This AI got me a date" posts - organic sharing
3. Referral program - 1 free month per referral
4. Anonymous testimonials - Reddit success stories

**Viral Coefficient:**
- 40% of users share results
- Each share reaches 500 people
- 2% convert from shares
- K = 0.4 × 500 × 0.02 = 0.4 (need improvement)

**Improvements:**
- In-app sharing to TikTok (one-click)
- Referral bonuses (free Pro month)
- Viral challenges ("I let AI write my messages for a week")

## Unit Economics

### Subscription Model

**Acquisition (CAC):**
- TikTok/IG Reels: $15
- Reddit communities: $10
- Referrals: $5 (1 free month cost)
- **Blended CAC: $25**

**Revenue (ARPU):**
- Free users: $0 (60%)
- Pro: $19/month (30%)
- VIP: $39/month (10%)
- **Blended ARPU: $15**

**Retention & LTV:**
- Avg dating app usage: 6 months
- Monthly churn: 15%
- **LTV: $15 × 6 = $90**

**Gross Margin:**
- API costs: $2 per user/month
- Infrastructure: $1 per user/month
- **COGS: $3 (20%)**
- **Gross Margin: 80%**

**Payback Period:**
- CAC: $25
- Gross profit per user: $12/month
- Payback: 2.1 months

## Upsell Opportunities

1. **Premium AI models** - Access to GPT-4 quality
2. **Human review** - Real dating coach feedback on request
3. **Dating coaching** - Partner with human coaches, 30% commission
4. **Photography** - Partner with photographers for dating photos
5. **Style consulting** - Partner with stylists

## Churn Mitigation

**Problem:** Users leave when they find relationships

**Solutions:**
1. **Wedding referrals** - users recommend to single friends
2. **Reactivation campaigns** - "Back on the market? Welcome back with 50% off"
3. **Expanded features** - help with messaging after matches (conversation management)
4. **Alumni program** - success stories, testimonials
5. **Broaden beyond dating** - social skills coaching

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Messages rewritten per user** (target: 10+/week)
2. **Reply rate improvement** (target: +30%)
3. **Match conversion rate** (target: +20%)
4. **Dates secured** (self-reported, target: +25%)
5. **Retention** (target: 6 month avg)
6. **NPS** (target: >60)
7. **Viral coefficient** (target: >1.0)

## Ethics Considerations

**Potential concern:** "Is using AI in dating cheating?"

**Response framework:**
- Position as "communication coach" not "deception"
- Focus on authenticity - AI helps you sound like your best self
- Transparency - encourage users to be honest about who they are
- Success stories - emphasize genuine connections, not manipulation
