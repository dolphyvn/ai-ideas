# Earnings Claim Fraud Detector - AI-Powered FDD Earnings Claim Validation

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Status:** GO - 8.4/10

## Core Concept

AI-powered platform that validates franchise earnings claims in FDDs against actual franchisee financial data. Flags statistically implausible claims, cross-references Item 19 disclosures with franchisee surveys/tax data proxies, and generates risk reports for attorney review before the FTC investigates.

**The Bridge:** Manual Earnings Claim Review (attorneys bill $400+/hour, impossible to validate) → [AI] - Automated Claim Validation (statistical analysis + pattern detection)
