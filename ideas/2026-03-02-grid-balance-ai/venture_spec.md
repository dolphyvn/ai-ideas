## Core Concept

AI-powered interconnection queue management platform that tracks renewable energy project queues across utilities, predicts approval timelines, identifies bottlenecks, and manages application compliance. The system scrapes public queue data, learns approval patterns, estimates project completion dates, and alerts developers to queue position changes.

**The Bridge:** Manual Queue Tracking (spreadsheets, uncertainty) → [AI] → Predictive Timeline Management (visibility, alerts)

## Problem Statement

### The Interconnection Bottleneck

- **50,000+ renewable projects** stuck in interconnection queues
- **2-5 year wait times** for grid connection approval
- **Projects die waiting** - Developers lose millions in delay costs
- **No transparency** - Developers don't know queue position or timeline
- **Compliance complexity** - Each utility has different requirements
