# Bar Ethics Monitor - Revenue Model

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Subscription

| Tier | Monthly | Annual (2 months free) | Target |
|------|---------|----------------------|--------|
| Starter | $1,000 | $10,000 | Firms 10-50 attorneys |
| Professional | $2,500 | $25,000 | Firms 50-200 attorneys |
| Enterprise | Custom | $100K+ | AmLaw 200, 200+ attorneys |

### Seat-Based Add-Ons

| Add-on | Price | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Additional attorney | $20/mo | Beyond base tier |
| Paralegal access | $10/mo | Read-only |
| API access | $500/mo | For matter system integration |
| White-glove onboarding | $5K one-time | Dedicated data engineer |

## Unit Economics

### CAC Breakdown

| Channel | Cost | Time to Close |
|---------|------|---------------|
| Outbound sales | $8K | 3 months |
| Inbound organic | $2K | 1 month |
| Partner referrals | $1K | 2 months |
| **Blended** | **$5K** | **2.5 months** |

### LTV Calculation

```
ARPU: $2,000/mo (blended)
Gross retention: 90%
Net retention: 105% (upsell)
Customer lifetime: 3 years

LTV = $2,000 × 36 × 0.9 = $64,800
LTV/CAC = 13x
Payback = 2.5 months
```

## Expansion Revenue

### Upsell Paths
1. **Tier upgrades**: Starter → Professional (+$1,500/mo)
2. **State expansion**: $500/mo per additional state bundle
3. **Module add-ons**:
   - Corporate family trees: +$1,000/mo
   - Alumni tracker: +$500/mo
   - Rules engine: +$500/mo

### Churn Mitigation
- Annual contracts (with discount)
- Matter-level integration (switching cost)
- Audit trail exports (data lock-in)
- Ethics partner certification (credentialing)

## Pricing Psychology

### Anchor High
- Lead with Enterprise pricing ($100K+)
- Makes Professional look like bargain
- Firms expect legal tech to be expensive

### Value-Based Framing
- "Cost of one missed conflict: $500K+"
- "Associate hours saved: 200 hours/year"
- "Malpractice premium reduction: 10%"

### Procurement Navigation
- SOC 2 Type II (security requirement)
- Bar association endorsements (trust signal)
- E&O insurance coverage (risk mitigation)
- Flexible billing (quarterly for government contracts)
