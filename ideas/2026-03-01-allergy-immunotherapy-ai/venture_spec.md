# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**AllergyImmunotherapyAI - Allergy Immunotherapy Documentation Platform**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered platform for allergists that automates allergy testing documentation, immunotherapy shot records, prior authorization for biologic treatments (Xolair), and patient communication.

## Problem:
- **5,000+ allergists** in US
- **Xolair cost**: $10K-$30K annually (biologic for asthma, hives, eczema)
- **Prior authorization burden**: 70%+ of Xolair prescriptions require prior auth
- **Immunotherapy records**: 3-5 years of allergy shots = complex recordkeeping
- **Allergy testing documentation**: Skin prick tests, IgE levels, component testing
- **Patient communication**: Allergy shots = recurring visits (weekly/bi-weekly)

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** Allergy Practices
- Allergists (MD/DO)
- Allergy immunotherapy clinics
- ENT physicians (with allergy focus)
- Pediatric allergists

**Secondary:** Allergy patients, allergy testing labs, biologic manufacturers

## Why Now:
1. **Allergy epidemic**: 50M+ Americans with allergies (growing)
2. **Biologic expansion**: Xolair, Dupixent, Nucala = new biologic options
3. **Prior auth growth**: Insurers requiring prior auth for allergy biologics
4. **Immunotherapy growth**: Allergy shots = increasing (effectiveness proven)
5. **Telemedicine allergy**: Tele-allergy visits = COVID-accelerated

## AI Advantage:
- **Allergy testing documentation**: AI generates allergy test reports (skin prick, IgE, component)
- **Immunotherapy tracking**: AI tracks shot records, vial expiration, dosing schedules
- **Xolair prior auth**: AI completes biologic prior auth (IgE levels, severity criteria)
- **Shot reminder**: AI reminds patients of shot schedules, manages vial inventory
- **Allergy severity scoring**: AI calculates severity scores (SNOT, TNSS, TSS)
- **Patient education**: AI generates allergy education materials, avoidance strategies

## Viral Mechanism:
- **AAAAI conferences**: American Academy of Allergy, Asthma & Immunology
- **ACAAI conferences**: American College of Allergy, Asthma & Immunology
- **Allergy patient community**: Seasonal allergies = MASSIVE suffering
- **Case studies**: "Got Xolair approved 2x faster using AI"
- **Allergy shot patients**: "Never missed a shot using AI reminders"

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Practice Subscription:**
- **Solo**: $800/month - 1-3 allergists, immunotherapy tracking
- **Practice**: $2,000/month - 4+ allergists, prior auth + allergy testing
- **Enterprise**: $5,000/month - Multi-location + patient portal + API

**Per-Patient add-on:**
- $5 per immunotherapy patient per month (beyond included tiers)

**Implementation:**
- $3,000 one-time (shot record setup, allergy testing integration, training)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $120 (AAAAI, ACAAI conferences)
- LTV: $28,800 (36-month retention - switching costs moderate)
- Gross Margin: 85%

## Moat:
1. **Allergy expertise**: Skin prick testing, IgE levels, component testing, immunotherapy protocols
2. **Xolair prior auth**: Biologic prior auth (IgE levels, asthma severity criteria)
3. **Immunotherapy records**: Shot records (3-5 years), vial tracking, dosing schedules
4. **Allergy severity scores**: SNOT (Sino-Nasal Outcome Test), TNSS (Total Nasal Symptom Score), TSS (Total Symptom Score)
5. **Vial inventory**: Immunotherapy vials = expiration tracking, lot numbers

## MVP in 12 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-3:**
- Focus: Immunotherapy tracking only
- ONE allergy practice pilot
- Manual workflow

**Weeks 4-6:**
- Build AI immunotherapy tracker (shot records, vial inventory)
- Shot reminder system (patient notifications)
- Allergy severity calculator (SNOT, TNSS)

**Weeks 7-9:**
- Partner with 5 allergy practices
- Deploy pilot for immunotherapy tracking
- Validate: Shot compliance, time savings

**Weeks 10-12:**
- Add Xolair prior auth module
- Allergy testing documentation
- Prepare AAAAI/ACAAI conference demo

**Validation Metrics:**
- 90%+ shot compliance (no missed shots)
- 80%+ reduction in immunotherapy admin time
- 3+ practices commit after pilot
- One Xolair prior auth approved using AI

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: GPT-4 (prior auth completion, patient education), Custom ML (severity scoring)
- **Integrations**: Allergy testing labs, EHR systems, patient portals
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (US regions, HIPAA compliant)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $8K MRR (10 practices × $800 ARPU)
- **Year 2**: $40K MRR (50 practices + practice tier)
- **Year 3**: $120K MRR (150 practices + enterprise)

## Risk Factors:
1. **Prior auth denials**: Insurers may tighten allergy biologic criteria
2. **Allergy shot decline**: Immunotherapy = less popular (oral immunotherapy growing)
3. **Competition**: Allergy EHRs (Pulse, EMA) adding features
4. **Seasonal variability**: Allergy = seasonal (spring, fall) = revenue variability
5. **Xolair competition**: Dupixent, Nucala (other biologics) = complexity

## Competitive Threat:
- **Allergy EHRs**: Pulse (Modernizing Medicine), EMA Derm (not allergy-specific)
- **Immunotherapy software**: AllergyEars, ShotSpotter (patient-focused, not practice)
- **Prior auth platforms**: SamaCare (oncology focus), CoverMyMeds (general)

**Differentiation**: Only AI platform for allergists with immunotherapy tracking, Xolair prior auth, and allergy severity scoring.
