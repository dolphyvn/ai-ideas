# RightsBackAI - Employee-Facing Wage Rights Platform

## Venture Spec

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Status:** GO - 8.3/10
**Category:** HR Tech / Consumer Legal Tech / B2B2C

## Core Concept

Employee-facing wage rights platform. Workers answer questions about their pay situation; AI generates demand letters, DOL complaints, and legal memos. Employers receiving letters upgrade to enterprise platform for monitoring and prevention. Classic "freemium defense" viral loop.

**The Bridge:** Employee Wage Claims (expensive lawyers, complex process) → [AI] → Free Self-Service Tools (demand letters, complaints) → Employer Buys Defense Platform

---

## Problem Statement

### The Wage Theft Crisis (Employee Side)

- **$50B+ in wages stolen annually** (EPI estimate)
- **Lawyers won't take small claims** - contingent fee only on large cases
- **DOL complaint process is opaque** - employees don't know how to file
- **Employees lack resources** - can't afford $400/hour employment lawyers
- **Employers count on silence** - most violations never reported

### The Current Reality (Employee)

```
"My boss stopped paying overtime last year but I'm scared to complain.
I can't afford a lawyer and don't know if I have a case. I'd use a free
tool that checked my rights and wrote a demand letter for me. Even better
if it filed the DOL complaint automatically."
- Anonymous Employee, Retail Worker
```

### The Current Reality (Employer)

```
"We got a demand letter from an employee - clearly AI-generated but
legally sound. We hired outside counsel at $500/hour to respond.
We'd pay $1,000/month for a platform that told us when we're at risk
and helped us fix issues before they become demand letters."
- Owner, Restaurant Chain
```

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities (Free Tier):**
- Rights analysis (FLSA, state wage laws, local ordinances)
- Demand letter generation (legally sound, properly formatted)
- DOL complaint drafting (agency-specific requirements)
- Small claims court filings (where applicable)
- Statute of limitations calculator

**Core Capabilities (Employer Tier):**
- Demand letter monitoring (alerts when employees generate letters)
- Risk assessment (what prompted the claim, systemic issues)
- Remediation recommendations (specific fixes to prevent escalation)
- "RightsBack Certified" badge (recruitment signal)

**Defensibility:**
- **Network effects** - Employee complaint database = early warning
- **First-mover advantage** - Employee tool becomes standard
- **Employer switching costs** - Ongoing monitoring essential
