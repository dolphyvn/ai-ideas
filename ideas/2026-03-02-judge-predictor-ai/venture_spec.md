## Core Concept

AI-powered judicial outcome prediction platform that analyzes judge's ruling history on similar motions, case types, and fact patterns to provide data-driven win probability scores for litigation strategy decisions. Ingests judge's entire PACER ruling history and appellate reversals to provide predictions with confidence intervals.

**The Bridge:** Intuitive Judge "Lore" → [AI] → Data-Driven Judge Intelligence (outcome predictions, reversal rates, pattern recognition)
