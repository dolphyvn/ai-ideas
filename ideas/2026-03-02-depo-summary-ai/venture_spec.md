## Name

**DepoSummaryAI - Automated Deposition Transcript Analysis for Litigation Teams**

---

## Core Concept

AI-powered deposition transcript summarization platform that analyzes testimony, extracts key admissions, flags inconsistencies, identifies privilege risks, and generates searchable transcript databases. The system processes hundreds of pages of transcripts in minutes, highlights critical testimony, and enables attorneys to find information across depositions.

**The Bridge:** Manual Transcript Review (4-8 hours per deposition) → [AI] → Automated Analysis (minutes, comprehensive)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Deposition Review Bottleneck

- **100,000+ litigation firms** handle civil litigation with depositions
- **50-200 page transcripts** per deposition take 4-8 hours to review
- **Attorney time costs $400-800/hour** - Manual review is expensive
- **Key testimony buried** - Critical admissions missed in lengthy transcripts
- **Inconsistency detection manual** - Comparing testimony across witnesses is error-prone
