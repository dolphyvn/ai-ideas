# Revenue Model: CPA Shield

## Pricing Strategy

### Tier 1: Small Firms (2-20 CPAs)
**Price:** $299/month or $2,990/year (2 months free)
**Target:** 9,000 small accounting firms in US
**Features:**
- Up to 20 CPAs tracked
- All 50 states covered
- CPE requirement tracking
- License expiration alerts (90, 60, 30 days)
- Basic dashboard
- Email support

### Tier 2: Mid-Market Firms (20-100 CPAs)
**Price:** $799/month or $7,990/year (2 months free)
**Target:** 12,000 mid-market firms in US
**Features:**
- Up to 100 CPAs tracked
- Everything in Small Firm tier
- CPE course recommendations
- Multi-state license mapping
- Firm-wide compliance reporting
- Priority support
- API access (read-only)

### Tier 3: Large Firms (100-500 CPAs)
**Price:** $2,499/month or $24,990/year (2 months free)
**Target:** 3,000 large firms in US
**Features:**
- Up to 500 CPAs tracked
- Everything in Mid-Market tier
- Custom state board rule alerts
- Compliance workflow automation
- Dedicated account manager
- Full API access (read/write)
- Onboarding assistance
- Quarterly compliance review

### Tier 4: Enterprise (500+ CPAs)
**Price:** Custom, typically $50,000-200,000/year
**Target:** Big 4 + top 100 firms
**Features:**
- Unlimited CPAs
- Everything in Large Firm tier
- Custom integrations (Caseware, Thomson Reuters)
- White-label options
- On-premise deployment option
- 24/7 support
- Custom SLAs

### Individual CPA (B2C)
**Price:** $19/month or $159/year
**Target:** 600,000 CPAs in US
**Note:** Free if their firm subscribes (firm-sponsored accounts)
**Features:**
- Single CPA tracking
- All 50 states covered
- CPE gap analysis
- License expiration alerts
- Mobile app access

## Revenue Streams

### Primary: Subscription Revenue
- **Expected mix:** 70% firm subscriptions, 30% individual (mostly firm-sponsored)
- ** churn target:** <5% monthly for firms (high switching costs), <10% for individuals

### Secondary: CPE Provider Partnerships
- **Model:** Revenue share (20-30%) on course completions
- **Value:** CPE providers pay for qualified leads (CPA who needs specific ethics course)
- **Potential:** $10-50K MRR at scale

### Tertiary: Audit Defense Add-on
- **Model:** $0/month monitoring, $10,000 emergency fee
- **Trigger:** When CPA faces state board inquiry/complaint
- **Service:** Connect with specialized attorney, coordinate response, document preparation
- **Margin:** 70% (we keep $7K, pay $3K to attorney partner)

- **Small firms:** $500 (cold email + LinkedIn ads)

### Payback Period
- **Small firms:** 1.7 months ✅

## Pricing Rationale

### Why Firms Will Pay $799+/month
1. **Alternative cost:** Firms spend $5,000+/year per CPA on compliance (manual tracking, software, admin time)
2. **Risk reduction:** One compliance incident can cost $100K+ in penalties + lost clients
3. **Partner liability:** Partners personally sign off on firm compliance, highly motivated
4. **Current pain:** Spreadsheets are error-prone, existing solutions are clunky enterprise suites

### Why $299 for Small Firms
- Small firms have tight budgets but still face same compliance burden
- Low price point for rapid market penetration
- Upsell path as firms grow

### Why $19 for Individuals
- Low enough for impulse purchase
- High enough to imply value
- Most individuals will be firm-sponsored (zero marginal cost)
