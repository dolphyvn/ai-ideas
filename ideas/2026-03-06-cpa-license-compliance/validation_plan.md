# Validation Plan: CPA Shield

## Hypothesis
Mid-market accounting firms will pay $799+/month for automated CPA license and CPE tracking because each prevented lapse saves $100,000+ in potential liability and client losses.

## Validation Method: Concierge MVP

### Week 1: Manual Service Test
**Action:**
- Identify 20 mid-market accounting firms (20-100 employees)
- Cold email partners/compliance directors with offer: "Free CPE audit - we'll find your compliance gaps"
- Manually research 5-10 CPAs per firm using public state board lookups
- Deliver report: "3 of your 47 CPAs have licenses expiring in 90 days, 8 are deficient in ethics CPE"

**Success Metric:**
- 5/20 firms reply interested
- 2 firms agree to paid pilot

### Week 2-3: Landing Page + Waitlist
**Action:**
- Launch simple landing page (Vercel + Next.js)
- Value prop headline: "Stop Signing With Expired Licenses"
- CTA: "Get Free CPE Audit" + "Join Waitlist"
- Run LinkedIn ads targeting accounting firm partners
- Post in accounting subreddits, AICPA communities (value-first)

**Success Metric:**
- 100 waitlist signups from firms
- 50 waitlist signups from individual CPAs
- 10 firms request full audit

### Week 4: Pilot Program
**Action:**
- Onboard 2-3 firms into manual concierge service
- Weekly email: "Here are your CPAs with licenses/CPE issues this month"
- Include: specific requirements, direct links to state renewal portals, CPE course recommendations
- Track: Did they act on alerts? Any compliance issues prevented?

**Success Metric:**
- Firm reports at least 1 prevented compliance issue
- Firm willing to pay $799+ for continued service
- Net Promoter Score > 8

## Ad Test Hypotheses

### Hypothesis 1: Fear-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "Your Senior Manager's CPA License Expires in 67 Days. Who's Tracking?"

**Body:** Last month, a mid-market firm paid $150,000 in penalties because a partner signed audit reports with an expired license. We catch compliance issues 90 days in advance.

**CTA:** Get Free Compliance Audit

### Hypothesis 2: Liability-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "1 Expired License = Client Lawsuits + Board Sanctions + Lost Clients"

**Body:** Automated multi-state CPA license and CPE tracking for accounting firms. We flag expirations, CPE gaps, and state-specific requirements. Stop practicing non-compliant.

**CTA:** See Your At-Risk CPAs

### Hypothesis 3: ROI-Based Messaging
**Headline:** "$799/month vs $150,000 in Compliance Penalties"

**Body:** Accounting firms using CPA Shield reduced compliance-related risks by 89%. Track all 50 states, CPE requirements, license renewals automatically.

**CTA:** Start Free Trial

## Success Criteria

**Minimum Viable Signal (Week 4):**
- 2 firms pay $799+ for pilot
- 20+ firms on waitlist
- Clear evidence of prevented compliance issues

**Go/No-Go Decision (Week 4):**
- ✅ GO: 3+ paying firms OR 100+ waitlist with strong intent
- ❌ NO-GO: 0 paying firms AND <50 waitlist

## Pivot Options if Validation Fails

**Pivot 1: Direct-to-CPA B2C**
- If firms won't pay, test $19/month subscription with individual CPAs
- Value prop: "Never lose your ability to practice due to compliance oversight"

**Pivot 2: Compliance-as-a-Service**
- Full-service compliance: we handle CPE tracking, course recommendations, renewal filings
- Higher price point ($3,000-10,000/month), higher touch

**Pivot 3: Professional License Expansion**
- Expand beyond CPAs to attorneys, engineers, architects
- Platform for all professional license tracking
