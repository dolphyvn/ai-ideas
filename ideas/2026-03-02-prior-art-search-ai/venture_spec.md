## Core Concept

AI-powered patent prior art search platform that searches global patent databases, academic literature, and product listings to predict patentability and assess freedom to operate. The system uses semantic search across millions of patents/papers, analyzes claim coverage, generates comprehensive prior art reports, and estimates patentability strength.
