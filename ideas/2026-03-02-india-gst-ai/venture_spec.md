## 2. Core Concept

AI-powered SaaS platform for Indian tax professionals that automates:

- **GST Return Preparation:** Automated GSTR-1, GSTR-3B preparation from accounting data
- **Compliance Checking:** ML-based error detection and validation before filing
- **Audit Risk Prediction:** SAT (Scrutiny, Audit, Trial) risk scoring based on historical data
- **Input Tax Credit (ITC) Reconciliation:** Automated matching of claimed vs. eligible ITC
- **E-way Bill Anomaly Detection:** Tax evasion risk identification
- **Tax Controversy Management:** Dispute outcome prediction and case management

## 3. Problem Statement

### Market Size

- **100,000+** tax professionals in India (Chartered Accountants, tax practitioners)
- **13M+** active GST registrations (massive compliance burden)
- **INR 14.5 Lakh Crore** (~$175B) in annual GST collections (FY 2024-25)
- **5%** of GST registrants audited annually (650,000+ audits)

### GST Complexity

India's GST system is uniquely complex:

1. **Five Tax Slabs:** 0%, 5%, 12%, 18%, 28% (plus cess on luxury/sin goods)
2. **Dual Structure:** CGST + SGST (intrastate) vs IGST (interstate)
3. **Input Tax Credit:** Chain mechanism with complex eligibility rules
4. **Reverse Charge:** Tax payable on receipt of specified goods/services
5. **E-way Bills:** Mandatory for interstate movement > INR 50,000
6. **Multiple Returns:** GSTR-1 (outward supplies), GSTR-3B (summary), GSTR-9 (annual)

### Compliance Burden

| Frequency | Return | Purpose |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Monthly | GSTR-1 | Outward supplies details |
| Monthly | GSTR-3B | Summary tax return |
| Quarterly | GSTR-4 | Composition scheme |
| Annually | GSTR-9 | Annual return |
| Event-based | E-way bill | Goods movement |

### Pain Points

1. **Time-Consuming Manual Work:** 20-40 hours per month per practitioner on GST compliance
2. **Error-Prone:** Manual data entry leads to filing errors (penalty + interest)
3. **Audit Anxiety:** Increasing GST audits, no visibility into audit risk
4. **ITC Reconciliation:** Manual matching is nightmare (thousands of invoices)
5. **E-way Bill Mismatches:** Common trigger for GST audits
6. **Tax Controversy:** No visibility into likely outcomes of disputes

### Consequences

- **Penalties:** 10% of tax (minimum INR 10,000) for late filing
- **Interest:** 18% per annum on delayed tax payment
- **Prosecution:** Criminal prosecution for tax evasion > INR 2.5 Crore
- **Business Disruption:** GST audits disrupt operations

## 6. AI Advantage

### 6.1 GST Return Automation

**Input:** Accounting data (Tally, Zoho, Excel)

**AI Processing:**
- Invoice classification (taxable/exempt/nil-rated)
- Tax rate determination (5/12/18/28% + cess)
- CGST/SGST vs IGST classification
- Place of supply rules (interstate vs intrastate)

**Output:** Draft GSTR-1, GSTR-3B ready for review

### 6.2 Compliance Checking ML

**Error Detection:**
- Mismatched tax rates
- Missing invoices
- Duplicate ITC claims
- RCM (Reverse Charge) identification failures
- Exemption misuse

**Validation Rules:**
- 500+ GST notification rules
- Section-specific validations
- APaaS (Advanced Ruling) precedents

**Accuracy Target:** 95%+ error detection

### 6.3 Audit Risk Prediction (SAT Scoring)

**Features:**
- Historical audit triggers
- Industry benchmarking
- Geographic risk factors (state-wise enforcement)
- ITC claim patterns
- E-way bill anomalies

**Output:**
- Risk score (1-100)
- High-risk areas flagged
- Mitigation recommendations

**Training Data:**
- Publicly available GST audit orders
- CBIC circulars
- State GST department enforcement patterns

### 6.4 Input Tax Credit Reconciliation

**Challenge:** Matching claimed ITC vs. supplier-filed GSTR-1

**AI Approach:**
- Fuzzy matching (invoice number variations)
- Supplier name normalization
- Tax amount variance detection
- Period mismatch identification

**ROI:** 80%+ time savings vs manual reconciliation

### 6.5 E-way Bill Anomaly Detection

**Red Flags:**
- Bill-to vs Consignee mismatch
- Vehicle number pattern anomalies
- Distance vs tax amount anomalies
- Route deviation detection
- Frequent cancellations

**Use Case:** Pre-filing validation for businesses

### 6.6 Tax Controversy Prediction

**Features:**
- Case category
- State jurisprudence trends
- Tribunal precedent analysis
- Bench strength analysis

**Output:** Win probability + settlement recommendation
