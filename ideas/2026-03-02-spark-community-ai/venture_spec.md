## Core Concept

AI-powered community automation agent that launches and nurtures pre-launch communities across multiple channels while founders sleep. The platform identifies where target customers hang out (Discord, Reddit, X, LinkedIn), crafts platform-native value-add content, engages authentically to drive waitlist signups, and nurtures early adopters with personalized check-ins.

**The Bridge:** Community Strategy (Static Document) → [AI] → Community Execution (Automated Engagement)

## Problem Statement

### The Pre-Launch Community Gap

- **Community-led growth is the new PLG** - But building communities manually is exhausting
- **Pre-launch communities are critical** - But founders lack time to engage across 5+ channels daily
- **Community tools exist but don't ENGAGE** - Circle, Discord, Slack host but don't do the work
- **Cross-channel chaos** - Each platform has different norms, formats, posting patterns
- **Inconsistent engagement** - Manual community building lasts 2-3 weeks before burnout

### The Current Reality

```
"I tried to build a community before launching my SaaS product. I set up
a Discord server, posted in 5 subreddits, engaged on Twitter daily,
and participated in 3 LinkedIn groups. It lasted 3 weeks before I

I spent 20 hours/week for 3 weeks and had nothing to show for it."
- Solo founder, after abandoned community attempt
```
