## Core Concept

AI-powered IRS audit defense platform for small CPA firms. Analyzes audit notices, generates defense strategies, drafts response letters, and predicts settlement outcomes based on historical Tax Court decisions. Uses public audit data (Tax Court decisions, revenue rulings, CP notices) plus private audit history.

**The Bridge:** Manual Audit Defense (expensive outsourcing, slow) → [AI] → Automated Defense Strategy (public precedent + outcome prediction)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Audit Defense Crisis

- **500K+ tax professionals** face client audits annually
- **Audit defense costs**: $500-5,000 per audit (outsourced)
- **Settlement outcomes unpredictable**: No way to gauge likely outcome
- **Small CPA firms** lack audit defense expertise (Big 4 have internal teams)
- **IRS enforcement up 40%** since 2021

### The Current Reality

```
"Our firm handles 30-40 client audits annually. We outsource defense
to specialists at $200-400/hour because we lack in-house expertise.
Each audit takes 3-6 months and costs clients $5,000-50,000. We
have no way to predict outcomes or compare to similar cases. We'd pay
$3,000/year for AI that provides defense strategies and predicts
settlement ranges based on actual case outcomes."
- Managing Partner, Regional CPA Firm
```
