## Core Concept

AI-powered construction punch list automation that uses computer vision to classify defect photos, prioritize issues by severity, track completion status, and generate comprehensive punch lists. The system processes site photos, identifies defects, assigns accountability, and integrates with construction workflows without requiring full PM system integration.

**The Bridge:** Manual Punch List Process (spreadsheets, disconnected photos) → [AI] → Automated Defect Tracking (computer vision, prioritized)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Punch List Bottleneck

- **$50B+ annually** spent on punch list work in US construction
- **3-6% of project budget** consumed by punch list items
- **Thousands of photos** per project with no organization

- **Items fall through cracks** - unclear accountability, delayed completion
