**Date:** 2026-03-02

## Core Concept

AI-powered SEC exam preparation and defense platform for RIAs. Uses ML trained on SEC enforcement actions and proprietary exam outcome data to predict deficiencies before exams arrive. Generates mock exam simulations, document request checklists, and defense preparedness reports.

**The Bridge:** Manual SEC Exam Prep (200+ hours, consultant fees $25K-100K) → [AI] → Predictive Deficiency Detection (exam-ready before notice arrives)

---

## Problem Statement

### The SEC Exam Crisis

- **SEC exams every 3-6 years** = sword of Damocles for RIAs
- **200+ hours of document prep** per exam
- **40% of exams result in deficiencies** - public record
- **Average fines: $50K-500K** for serious deficiencies
- **Consultants charge $25K-100K** for exam prep
- **Deficiencies are public** = client attrition, reputation damage

### The Current Reality

```
"We received our SEC exam notice last week. Now we're scrambling
to gather 3 years of documents, interview staff, and prepare for
the on-site visit. We're paying our compliance consultant $75K
and still terrified they'll find something. We'd pay $10K immediately
for AI that told us our exact deficiency risks and prepared us
for what examiners will ask."
- CCO, Mid-Market RIA ($1.2B AUM)
```

- Enforcement actions up 40% since 2020
- Public deficiency database growing

### 2. Compliance Consultant Shortage

- Qualified CCOs scarce (30% open positions)

- Fees increasing due to demand

### 3. Exam Deficiencies Public

- SEC publishes deficiency details

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- Deficiency prediction (ML trained on SEC enforcement + user exam outcomes)
- Exam simulation (mock questions based on firm profile)
- Document request prediction (what examiners will ask for)
- Policy gap analysis (compare firm policies against SEC priorities)
- Benchmarking (peer deficiency patterns by AUM, business model)

**Defensibility:**
- **Proprietary exam outcome database** (users contribute anonymized results)
- **Network effects** (more users = better predictions)
- **SEC enforcement pattern ML** (trained on public enforcement + private outcomes)
