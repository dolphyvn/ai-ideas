# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**NursingEdAI - Nursing Education Compliance Platform**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered platform for nursing education programs that automates NCLEX preparation, clinical rotation tracking, accreditation reporting (CCNE/ACEN), and state board approval compliance.

## Problem:
- **2,500+ nursing programs** in US (ADN, BSN, MSN, DNP)
- **Nursing shortage**: 1.2M nurse vacancy by 2030 (AACN)
- **NCLEX pass rates**: Public reporting, accreditation requires minimum pass rates
- **Clinical rotation tracking**: Manual coordination, compliance documentation
- **Accreditation reporting**: CCNE/ACEN accreditation + state board approval
- **Faculty shortage**: Nursing faculty shortage limits program expansion

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** Nursing Education Programs
- Associate Degree in Nursing (ADN) programs
- Bachelor of Science in Nursing (BSN) programs
- Master of Science in Nursing (MSN) programs
- Doctor of Nursing Practice (DNP) programs

**Secondary:** Nursing schools (for-profit, non-profit), hospital-based nursing programs

## Why Now:
1. **Nursing shortage**: 1.2M nurse vacancy by 2030 = pressure to expand programs
2. **NCLEX changes**: Next Gen NCLEX (2023) = new format, new prep needed
3. **Accreditation pressure**: CCNE/ACEN increasing requirements
4. **Clinical site shortage**: Limited clinical sites = efficient tracking needed
5. **Public reporting**: NCLEX pass rates publicly reported (affects enrollment)

## AI Advantage:
- **NCLEX prep**: Adaptive learning (ML) based on student performance
- **Clinical rotation matching**: AI matches students to clinical sites based on specialty, availability
- **Accreditation reporting**: Auto-generates CCNE/ACEN reports from student data
- **NCLEX prediction**: ML predicts student NCLEX pass likelihood (early intervention)
- **Faculty workload**: AI reduces faculty administrative burden (clinical evaluation, documentation)
- **State board reporting**: Auto-fills state board approval forms

## Viral Mechanism:
- **NLN conferences**: National League for Nursing annual meeting
- **AACN conferences**: American Association of Colleges of Nursing
- **Nursing educator networks**: Tight community, word-of-mouth
- **Case studies**: "Improved NCLEX pass rate 20% using AI"
- **Enrollment boost**: Higher NCLEX pass rates = more applicants

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Program Subscription:**
- **ADN/BSN**: $2,000/month - Up to 500 students
- **Graduate**: $4,000/month - MSN/DNP programs, unlimited students
- **Multi-Campus**: $8,000/month - Multiple campuses + white-label + API

**Per-Student add-on:**
- $50 per student for NCLEX prep module (beyond included tiers)

**Implementation:**
- $10,000 one-time (student data import, clinical site setup, accreditation mapping)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $250 (NLN, AACN conferences, nursing school partnerships)
- LTV: $57,600 (36-month retention - switching costs high)
- Gross Margin: 80%

## Moat:
1. **Accreditation complexity**: CCNE/ACEN accreditation + state board approval
2. **NCLEX expertise**: Next Gen NCLEX (2023) = new format, new content
3. **Clinical rotation tracking**: Clinical site relationships, compliance documentation
4. **Student data**: Historical NCLEX performance, clinical site data
5. **Nursing education workflow**: Clinical evaluation, faculty documentation

## MVP in 12 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-3:**
- Focus: NCLEX prep only
- One nursing program pilot (BSN)
- Manual question bank, adaptive learning

**Weeks 4-6:**
- Build AI NCLEX prep engine (adaptive learning)
- NCLEX pass prediction model
- Student performance dashboard

**Weeks 7-9:**
- Partner with 3 nursing programs (ADN, BSN, MSN)
- Deploy pilot for NCLEX prep
- Validate: NCLEX pass rate improvement, time savings

**Weeks 10-12:**
- Add clinical rotation tracking
- CCNE/ACEN reporting module
- Prepare NLN/AACN conference demo

**Validation Metrics:**
- 20%+ NCLEX pass rate improvement
- 80%+ student engagement (vs. traditional prep)
- 2+ programs commit after pilot
- One CCNE/ACEN report submitted using AI

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: Custom ML (adaptive learning, pass prediction), GPT-4 (question generation)
- **Integrations**: NCLEX testing services, clinical site databases, accreditation systems
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (US regions, FERPA compliant)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $30K MRR (15 programs × $2K ARPU)
- **Year 2**: $150K MRR (75 programs + graduate programs)
- **Year 3**: $450K MRR (225 programs + multi-campus)

## Risk Factors:
1. **NCLEX changes**: Next Gen NCLEX may change again
2. **Accreditation changes**: CCNE/ACEN standards may evolve
3. **Clinical site relationships**: Clinical sites may resist tracking
4. **Competition**: Elsevier, ATI (existing NCLEX prep vendors)
5. **Faculty adoption**: Some faculty may resist AI

## Competitive Threat:
- **Elsevier**: NCLEX prep (Saunder's), not full platform
- **ATI**: Nursing education testing, not clinical tracking
- **Kaplan**: NCLEX prep, not accreditation reporting
- **UWorld**: NCLEX question bank, not AI-adaptive

**Differentiation**: Only AI platform for nursing education with NCLEX prep (adaptive learning), clinical rotation tracking, and accreditation reporting.
