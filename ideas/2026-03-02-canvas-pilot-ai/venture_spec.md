## Name

## Core Concept

AI-powered platform that transforms static business model canvases into automated hypothesis testing engines. Extracts testable assumptions from business models, designs customer interview scripts, builds landing page variants, routes traffic, synthesizes learning, and automatically updates the canvas with validated insights.

**The Bridge:** Business Concept (Canvas) → [AI] → Digital Execution (Automated Experiments)

## Problem Statement

### The Execution Gap in Business Model Validation

- **90% of startups fail** due to lack of product-market fit
- **Business Model Canvases capture hypotheses** but teams never systematically test them
- **The "Idea Validation" bottleneck** - founders know they should test assumptions but lack bandwidth
- **Consulting frameworks exist** (Lean Startup, Jobs-to-be-Done) but require human facilitation
- **Corporate innovation teams** suffer same fate - great slide decks, no validation

### The Current Reality

```
"Our team spent 2 days at an offsite building our Business Model Canvas.
```

```
CanvasPilotAI:
```

### The Validation Pipeline

```
Business Model Canvas Upload (PDF, image, text)
    ↓
AI Extracts Testable Hypotheses (value prop, customer segments, channels)
    ↓
Prioritizes by Risk/Impact (RICE scoring)
    ↓
Generates Experiment Designs (interview scripts, landing pages, surveys)
    ↓
Launches Automated Tests (routes traffic, schedules outreach)
    ↓
Collects Customer Feedback (interviews, landing page conversions)
    ↓
AI Synthesizes Learning (patterns, insights, recommendations)
    ↓
Updates Canvas (validated/invalidated assumptions)
    ↓
Next Experiment Recommendation (continuous learning loop)
```
