# Revenue Model: CleryAct Copilot

## Pricing Strategy

### Tier 1: Small Colleges & Community Colleges (<5K students)
**Price:** $45,000/year
**Target:** ~1,500 small institutions in US
**Features:**
- Automated crime classification (7 Clery crime types)
- Daily crime log automation (public formatting)
- Timely warning template generator
- Annual Security Report (ASR) data export
- Basic geospatial mapping
- Clery geography categorization
- Email support
- Monthly compliance report

### Tier 2: Mid-Size Universities (5K-20K students)
**Price:** $95,000/year
**Target:** ~1,000 mid-size institutions in US
**Features:**
- Everything in Small College tier
- Advanced pattern detection (serial incidents, hot spots)
- Interactive geospatial mapping (heat maps, clustering)
- CSA (Campus Security Authority) training module
- Priority support (4-hour response)
- Quarterly compliance review
- Mobile app integration (push notifications)
- Historical ASR comparison (year-over-year trends)

### Tier 3: Large Universities & Systems (20K+ students)
**Price:** $150,000/year
**Target:** ~500 large institutions/systems in US
**Features:**
- Unlimited campuses and users
- Everything in Mid-Size tier
- Multi-campus support
- API access (full read/write)
- On-site training (annual)
- Dedicated account manager
- 24/7 emergency support
- Custom crime categories (state-specific)
- Integration with campus CAD/RMS systems
- Emergency notification testing module
- Compliance consulting (2 hours/month)

### Implementation Fees

- **$15,000 one-time** - System integration, training, configuration
- **$10,000 per additional campus** - Multi-campus deployment

## Revenue Streams

### Primary: Institution Subscription (92% of revenue)
- **Expected mix:** 50% mid-size, 30% large, 20% small
- **Churn target:** <4% annually (high switching costs, regulatory necessity)
- **Expansion revenue:** 12%+ annual upsell (additional campuses, modules)

### Secondary: ASR Preparation Service (5% of revenue)
- **Model:** One-time service for non-subscribers or add-on
- **Price:** $30,000 per ASR (vs. $50,000+ consulting competitors)
- **Service:** Full ASR preparation, DOE-compliant export, review
- **Margin:** 75% (mostly automated with human review)
- **Foot-in-door:** Convert to subscription after ASR delivery

### Tertiary: CSA Training Module (3% of revenue)
- **Model:** Per-training fees or unlimited training subscription
- **Price:** $5,000 per training session OR $10,000/year unlimited
- **Service:** Automated CSA training + compliance tracking
- **Market:** 35+ CSAs per campus, annual training required

## Pricing Rationale

### Why Institutions Will Pay $45K-150K/year

1. **ASR automation:** 4 months of FTE time = $25-40K in labor savings
2. **Penalty avoidance:** $60,000+ per Clery violation
3. **Student safety:** Timely warnings issued faster = real safety impact
4. **Staff efficiency:** Clery officer often has other duties (Title IX, etc.)
5. **Audit preparation:** DOE Clery audits require extensive documentation

### ROI Calculation (Mid-Size University)

**Annual Costs:**
- CleryAct Copilot: $95,000/year
- Implementation (one-time): $15,000

**Annual Savings:**
- ASR labor: 4 months × $8,333/month = $33,000
- Daily log automation: 2 hours/day × $50/hour × 250 days = $25,000
- Timely warning efficiency: 5 warnings/year × 4 hours saved × $50/hour = $1,000
- Avoided consulting fees: $30,000 per ASR (external vs. internal)
- Potential fine avoidance: 1 prevented violation × $60,000 = $60,000
- **Total savings: $149,000/year**

**Net ROI:** ($149,000 - $95,000) / $95,000 = **57% first-year ROI**

### Pricing Psychology

- **Anchored to consulting:** $95,000 vs. $50,000 ASR consulting (2 ASRs = payback)
- **Staff cost alternative:** 1 FTE = $60-80K/year (salary + benefits)
- **Fine avoidance:** $60,000 per violation creates urgency
- **Safety impact:** Hard to quantify but emotionally powerful

## Pricing Iteration Strategy

### Start at Market Rate (Value-Based Pricing)
- Institutions pay $30-50K for ASR consulting
- Annual Clery officer salary: $60-80K
- Pricing positioned as "fraction of cost" vs. alternatives

### Volume Discounts
- Multi-campus institutions get per-campus discount
- Multi-year contracts get 10-15% discount
- System-wide deployments (5+ campuses) get 20% discount

### Geographic Pricing
- No geographic variation (national pricing)
- Simplifies sales, reduces pricing friction

### Add-On Modules (Future)
- Mobile app (+$15K/year)
- Emergency notification testing (+$20K/year)
- Custom reporting (+$10K/year)
- Consulting hours (+$200/hour or retainer)

## Competitive Pricing Position

| Competitor | Pricing | Positioning |
|------------|---------|-------------|
| **Manual Processes** | $0 (high labor cost) | Free but time-intensive (4 months ASR) |
| **Consultants** | $30-50K per ASR | Reactive, expensive, no ongoing value |
| **Maxient** | $10-20K/year | Student conduct focus, not Clery-specific |
| **CleryAct Copilot** | $45-150K/year | Clery-specific, automated, ongoing value |

### Why We Win on Price

- **vs. Manual:** Payback in first ASR automation (4 months labor saved)
- **vs. Consultants:** 2-3 ASRs = subscription payback, ongoing value
- **vs. Maxient:** 3-5x price but 10x Clery-specific value
- **vs. FTE:** 1.5x Clery officer cost but 5x the capability

## Seasonal Sales Strategy

### ASR Season (June-September)
- **Peak urgency** - October 1st ASR deadline
- **"Emergency ASR Help"** - Acceleration offer
- **Discount for early signup** - Get ASR done before fall semester

### Off-Season (October-May)
- **Focus on timely warnings** - Campus safety messaging
- **Pattern detection demos** - Show safety value year-round
- **CSA training sales** - Annual training requirement

### Conference Timing
- **IACLEA Annual** - June (pre-ASR season, maximum impact)
- **Regional IACLEA meetings** - Quarterly presence
- **Clery Compliance Conference** - Specialist audience
