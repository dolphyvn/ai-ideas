## Name

**CleryAct Copilot - AI-Powered Clery Act Crime Reporting & Timely Warning Automation**

---

## Core Concept

AI-powered platform for Clery Act compliance officers that automates daily crime log management, timely warning notifications, crime classification and geospatial mapping, annual security report generation, and provides compliance risk scoring based on DOJ patterns. Integrates with campus police systems, incident management platforms, and campus mobile apps.

---

## Problem Statement

### The Clery Act Compliance Burden

- **4,000+ colleges and universities** subject to Clery Act (Title IV funding requirement)
- **Daily crime log required** - Public log of all reported crimes
- **Timely warnings** - Must notify campus of serious ongoing threats
- **Annual Security Report (ASR)** - Due October 1st, 100+ pages of crime statistics
- **$60,000+ per violation** - DOE civil penalties
- **Loss of federal funding** = institutional death for Title IV violations

### The Clery Act Requirements

```
Clery Act Requirements (2024):
- Clery Compliance Officer required (1-2 FTE)
- Daily crime log (public, within 2 business days)
- Timely warnings for ongoing threats (immediate)
- Crime statistics by category (7 crime types, 4 locations)
- Annual Security Report (ASR) - due October 1
- Campus security authority (CSA) training
- Fire safety reporting (on-campus housing)
- Hate crime reporting
- Dating violence/domestic violence/stalking reporting
- Emergency notification testing

Enforcement:
- DOE can withhold federal funding (Title IV)
- Public violation listings (DOE Clery Act reports)
- Campus safety audits (expensive consulting engagements)
- Negative publicity (admissions impact)
```

### The Timely Warning Reality

| Scenario | Current Response | Risk |
|----------|------------------|------|
| **Armed robbery near campus** | Manual email draft 4-6 hours later | Students unaware, ongoing threat |
| **Serial assault pattern** | Discovered during ASR preparation (months later) | Missed prevention opportunity |
| **Hate crime spike** | Manual trend analysis (never happens) | Campus climate impact |

### The Clery Officer's Daily Struggle

```
"I receive crime reports from 35 different Campus Security Authorities:
police, RAs, student affairs, athletics, Greek life. Each uses different
formats. I manually compile the daily crime log (2-3 hours daily).
Timely warnings take hours to draft, approve, and distribute. For the
Annual Security Report, I spend 4 months manually categorizing crimes
by geospatial coordinates. Last year we missed a timely warning deadline
and were fined. Clery compliance is my full-time job, but I'm one person
for 30,000 students across 200+ buildings."
- Clery Compliance Officer, Large Public University
```
