## Core Concept

AI-powered IRS Form 990 analysis platform that enables grantmakers, researchers, and journalists to discover patterns across millions of nonprofit financial disclosures. The system ingests all public 990 filings, provides semantic search ("find nonprofits with >20% admin costs but declining programs"), flags anomalies, and generates benchmarking reports.

**The Bridge:** Manual 990 Review (PDF downloads, spreadsheet analysis) → [AI] → Semantic Search Across 2M+ Filings (instant insights, anomaly detection)
