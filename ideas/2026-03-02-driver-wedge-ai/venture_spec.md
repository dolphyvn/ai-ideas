## Core Concept

AI-powered DOT compliance screening and predictive safety scoring for truck driver recruitment. The system automates PQF (Pre-Employment Screening Program) queries, analyzes past violation patterns, cross-references with DOT drug testing clearinghouse, and predicts insurance impact for driver candidates.

**The Bridge:** Manual Driver Screening (20+ hours per candidate, fragmented systems) → [AI] → Automated Risk Assessment (unified FMCSA data, predictive scoring)

- **80K driver shortage** - carriers desperate to hire

- **7,248 out-of-service in 2025** for English proficiency (5x increase, expanding to 48 states by Jan 2026)
- **20+ hours manual screening** per candidate through FMCSA systems
- **Bad hires cost $15K+** in turnover, insurance increases, DOT violations

### The Current Reality

before we hire."
