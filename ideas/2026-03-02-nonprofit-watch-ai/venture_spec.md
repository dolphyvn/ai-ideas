## Core Concept

AI-powered platform that benchmarks nonprofit governance risk across foundations' grant portfolios. Analyzes 2M+ Form 990 filings to detect red flags (excessive admin costs, executive pay spikes, compliance gaps) before foundations award grants. Provides peer benchmarking reports and "bad grant insurance."

**The Bridge:** Manual Grant Due Diligence (spreadsheet analysis, no peer comparison) → [AI] → Portfolio Risk Intelligence (benchmarking FOMO, red flag detection)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Foundation Due Diligence Crisis

- **Foundation boards personally liable** for prudent distribution
- **30% of grants have undisclosed red flags** (excessive admin costs, executive pay issues)
- **Manual 990 review takes 10-20 hours per nonprofit**
- **No peer benchmarking** - foundations can't compare portfolios
- **High-profile grant scandals** destroy foundation reputations

### The Current Reality

```
"Our board asked why our portfolio's admin costs are 22% when peers
average 15%. We had no answer - we'd never benchmarked. We spent
weeks manually reviewing 990s and still missed red flags that caused
two grants to fail. We'd pay $15K/year for AI that benchmarks our
portfolio against peers and catches issues BEFORE we award."
- Foundation CEO, $500M endowment
```

## Why Now

### 1. Foundation Accountability Rising

- Press scrutiny of foundation spending increasing
- "Effective altruism" movement demanding data-driven grantmaking
- State attorneys general enforcing prudent distribution rules

### 2. Public 990 Data Explosion

- 2M+ Form 990 filings machine-readable
- Vector database technology enables semantic search
- No platform has systematically analyzed this data

### 3. Board Liability Real

- Foundation directors personally liable for bad grants
- High-profile failures (e.g., FTX Foundation fallout) increase caution
- Boards demand due diligence tools

### 4. Benchmarking FOMO Viral

- "Why are we worse than peers?" = powerful motivator
- Foundation networks share benchmarking data
- Competitive anxiety drives adoption

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- Portfolio benchmarking (admin costs, overhead ratios vs. peers)
- Red flag detection (executive pay spikes, compliance gaps, unusual expenses)
- Peer group analysis (by mission type, budget, geography)
- Anomaly detection (outliers vs. similar organizations)
- Risk scoring (predictive likelihood of grant failure)

**Defensibility:**
- **Accumulated 990 intelligence** (vector DB of 2M+ filings)
- **Benchmarking database** (peer comparison data grows with users)
- **Network effects** (more foundations = better benchmarking)
