## Name

**FinancialCloseAI - Automated Month-End Close for Finance Teams**

## Core Concept

AI-powered month-end close automation that generates journal entries, calculates accruals, performs reconciliations, executes close checklists, and manages approval workflows. The system learns from company-specific patterns, predicts adjusting entries, identifies anomalies, and compresses close cycles from weeks to days.

**The Bridge:** Manual Month-End Close (10-14 days, sleepless nights) → [AI] → Automated Close (3-5 days, predictable)

### The Month-End Close Pipeline

```
Month-end trigger (calendar date)
    ↓
AI identifies recurring journal entries (depreciation, amortization, allocations)
    ↓
Generate journal entries for review (one-click approval workflow)
    ↓
Calculate accruals (rent, utilities, services - based on historical patterns)
    ↓
Generate accrual entries with documentation
    ↓
Execute reconciliations (bank accounts, credit cards, intercompany)
    ↓
Flag unreconciled items for human review
    ↓
Anomaly detection (unusual transactions, variance alerts)
    ↓
Close checklist execution (task assignments, approvals, signoffs)
    ↓
Consolidation (multi-entity, intercompany eliminations, currency translation)
    ↓
Financial statement generation (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow)
    ↓
Close complete (audit trail, SOX compliance, export to audit package)
    ↓
Learning feedback (this month's patterns train next month's automation)
```
