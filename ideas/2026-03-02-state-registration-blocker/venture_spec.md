# State Registration Blocker - Multi-State Franchise Registration Automation

**Date:** 2026-03-02

## Core Concept

AI-powered multi-state franchise registration platform. Tracks registration deadlines across 15+ filing states, quantifies revenue impact of missed deadlines, and automates renewal filings. "Permit-blocking" approach - cannot sell franchises in state without active registration.

**The Bridge:** Manual State Registration (spreadsheets, missed deadlines, lost sales) → [AI] - Automated Deadline Tracking (revenue impact quantification, filing automation)

---

## Problem Statement

### The State Registration Crisis

- **15+ states require franchise registration** before selling
- **Missed deadline = cannot sell franchises** in that state (revenue stops)
- **$1.5M average lost revenue** per year per missed state registration
- **Each state has different forms, fees, timing**
- **200+ hours/year** managing registrations manually

### The Current Reality

```
"We missed our California franchise registration deadline by 3 days.
The result: we couldn't sell franchises in California for 6 months,
costing us $4.2M in lost franchise fees. We'd pay $20K/year for a
system that ensured we never missed another deadline and told us
exactly what's at risk."
- VP Franchise Development, 200-Unit System
```
