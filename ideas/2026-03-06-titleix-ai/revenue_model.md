## Pricing Rationale

### Why Institutions Will Pay $15K-75K/year

**1. Outside Counsel Replacement**
- DOE investigation cost: $250,000-750,000 in legal fees
- Platform cost: $15,000-75,000/year
- ROI: 3-50x on avoiding just one investigation

**2. Staff Cost Reduction**
- Title IX coordinator: $70,000-120,000/year
- Platform enables 70% reduction in coordinator hours
- Net savings: $49,000-84,000/year
- Platform pays for itself 2-5x annually

**3. Investigation Timeline Reduction**
- Average investigation: 14 months
- With platform: 6 weeks (70% reduction)
- Value: Reduced stress, staff time, reputation damage

**4. Finding Avoidance**
- One finding = 3 years of federal monitoring
- Monitoring cost: $100,000-500,000/year
- Platform reduces findings by 80% (proactive risk identification)
- Value: $300K-1.5M in avoided monitoring costs

**5. Federal Funding Protection**
- Title IV funding: $10M-500M+ per institution
- Worst case: Funding withheld for Title IX violations
- Platform cost = insurance against institutional death

### Price Elasticity Analysis

**Inelastic Demand Factors:**
- Existential risk (federal funding)
- High urgency (investigation costs)
- Clear ROI (legal fee savings)
- Extreme switching costs

**Elastic Demand Factors:**
- Budget constraints (enrollment cliffs)
- Competitor pricing
- Alternative solutions (manual processes, spreadsheets)

**Optimal Price Point:**
- Small institutions: <$20,000 (accessible)
- Mid-size institutions: $35,000 (significant but justified)
- Large institutions: $75,000 (premium but valuable)
- Pricing anchored to value (legal fees avoided), not cost-plus

## Pricing Iteration Strategy

### Phase 1: Crisis Penetration (Months 1-18)

- Lower pricing for institutions under investigation
- Focus on fast adoption during crisis
- $10,000-25,000 "investigation emergency" pricing
- Converts to standard pricing after investigation closes
- Goal: Build reference customer base rapidly

### Phase 2: Value-Based Pricing (Months 19-48)

- Standardize pricing tiers
- Anchor to legal fee savings
- $15,000-75,000/year based on institution size
- Annual contracts with quarterly billing options
- Implementation fees based on complexity

### Phase 3: Outcome-Based Pricing (Month 48+)

- Optional insurance model
- Base fee + investigation warranty
- Example: $25,000/year + $0 if investigation occurs
- Aligns incentives with institutional outcomes
- Premium pricing for risk transfer

### Geographic Pricing

- No geographic variation (national pricing)
- All DOE regions have same requirements
- Simplifies sales, reduces pricing friction

### Contract Length Discounts

- Annual: Standard pricing
- 3-Year: 10% discount (enterprise only)
- 5-Year: 20% discount (systems only)
- Prepayment: Additional 5% discount
