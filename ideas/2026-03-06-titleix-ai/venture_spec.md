### The Defensibility of Job Displacement

**Why This Creates Competitive Moat:**

1. **DOE Outcome Data Network Effects:** Every investigation outcome trains the ML model. The platform learns what findings result from what patterns. New entrants cannot replicate this historical data.

2. **Investigation Workflow Lock-in:** Once institutions replace their manual processes with the automated workflow, reverting to spreadsheets and folders is impossible. The institutional knowledge of how to handle DOE investigations is now in the platform.

3. **Outside Counsel Dependency Elimination:** Institutions that eliminate outside counsel lose internal expertise. They become dependent on the platform for investigation competence. This creates extreme switching costs.

4. **Historical Data Asset:** Each institution's case history, investigation outcomes, and DOE interactions create a proprietary dataset. Leaving means abandoning years of compliance data.

5. **The "Point of No Return":** After 1-2 years using the platform, the institution has no staff who know how to manually manage Title IX compliance. The platform becomes mission-critical infrastructure.
