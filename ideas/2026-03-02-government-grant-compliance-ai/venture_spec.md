## Core Concept

AI-powered federal grant compliance platform. Automates SAM.gov registration monitoring, Single Audit preparation, Drawdown management, and CFR Title 2 compliance tracking. Prevents debarment (cannot receive ANY federal funds) through continuous compliance monitoring.

**The Bridge:** Manual Federal Grant Compliance (25% overhead, debarment risk) → [AI] - Automated Compliance Monitoring (permit-blocking for government grants)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Federal Grant Compliance Crisis

- **25% overhead** on federal grants for compliance reporting
- **SAM.gov registration expires annually** - inactive = cannot receive funds
- **Single Audit thresholds** create audit cliffs (expensive, time-consuming)
- **Debarment = business death** - cannot receive ANY federal funds
- **CFR Title 2 requirements** complex and constantly changing

### The Current Reality

```
"We lost $2M in federal funding because our SAM.gov registration lapsed
and we didn't realize until we tried to draw down funds. By the time we
renewed, the grant period ended. We also spent 400 hours preparing for
our Single Audit. We'd pay $15K/year for AI that monitored our federal
grant compliance and prevented debarment."
- CFO, Nonprofit Federal Grantee
```

## Why Now

### 1. Federal Grant Expansion

- Federal grants totaling $1T+ annually
- Infrastructure bill, IRA, CHIPS Act expanding grant programs
- More entities receiving federal funds than ever

### 2. SAM.gov "Permit-Blocking"

- Inactive SAM.gov registration = cannot receive payments
- Annual renewal requirement (easy to miss)
- No grace period - lapsed = immediate funding block

### 3. Debarment Risk Real

- OIG maintains debarment list
- Once debarred = 0 federal funds for 3+ years
- Business-ending for many grantees

### 4. Single Audit Complexity

- Single Audits cost $25K-100K and take 200+ hours
- Thresholds create cliff effects
- Noncompliance findings trigger audits

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- SAM.gov registration monitoring (expiration alerts, renewal automation)
- Single Audit preparation (CFR Title 2 compliance checking)
- Drawdown compliance (ensuring payments meet requirements)
- Debarment risk scoring (predictive based on compliance patterns)
- Award management (tracking requirements across multiple grants)

**Defensibility:**
- **SAM.gov integration complexity** (technical barrier)
- **Accumulated compliance patterns** (learn what triggers audits)
- **Multi-grant complexity** (organizations have 50+ grants simultaneously)
