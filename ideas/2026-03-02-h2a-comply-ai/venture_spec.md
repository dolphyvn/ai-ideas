## Core Concept

AI-powered H-2A agricultural visa program compliance automation system. The platform manages H-2A requirements including automated housing documentation, wage rate calculations, recruitment tracking, and compliance reporting. Uses public Department of Labor wage rate data and automates mandatory record-keeping for farms employing seasonal foreign workers.

**The Bridge:** Manual H-2A Management ($5-15K per worker in legal/admin fees) → [AI] → Automated Compliance (documentation, wage calculations, reporting)

---

## Problem Statement

### The H-2A Compliance Crisis

- **300K+ H-2A workers annually** (up from 200K in 2018 - 50% growth)
- **~10K farms using H-2A** (concentrated in CA, WA, FL, GA, NC)
- **Compliance costs: $5-15K per worker** in legal/admin fees
- **DOL penalties: up to $5K per worker** + potential debarment
- **Debarment = business-ending** (can't operate without labor)

### The Current Reality

```
"We employ 150 H-2A workers each season. The compliance paperwork
is overwhelming—housing documentation, wage calculations, recruitment
records, endless DOL reporting. We spend $10K per worker on legal
fees alone. One audit finding could debar us from the program
entirely, which would end our farm. We'd pay $50K annually for
AI that actually keeps us compliant."
- Farm Owner, 1,200-acre blueberry operation
```
