# Revenue Model: DebtDestroyer

## Core Value Proposition
Save thousands in interest, become debt-free years faster. $29/month for financial peace of mind.

## Pricing Strategy

### Freemium Model

**Free Tier:**
- Manual debt tracking
- Basic payoff calculator (snowball vs avalanche)
- Educational content
- Community access
- Purpose: Lead generation and user education

**Pro Tier - $29/month:**
- Automatic debt aggregation (Plaid)
- AI-optimized payoff strategy
- Extra payment calculator
- Progress visualization
- Email/payment reminders
- Early access to new features

**Premium Tier - $79/month:**
- Everything in Pro
- Rate negotiation (AI calls/chats with lenders)
- Balance transfer finder (best offers)
- Payment automation (auto-distribute extra payments)
- Priority support

**One-time options:**
- **Debt Plan** - $99 one-time
  - Custom strategy
  - Payoff timeline
  - No ongoing features
- **Success Package** - $99 when debt-free
  - Celebration assets
  - "Debt-free" certificate
  - Template for sharing story

## Alternative: Success-Based Pricing

**Pay What You Save:**
- Free during payoff period
- Pay 10% of interest saved when debt-free
- Capped at $999
- Verification required (before/after statements)

**Why offer both:** Attract risk-averse users while capturing more value from confident users.

**Payback Period:**
- CAC: $45

- Payback: 1.7 months

## Churn Mitigation

**Problem:** Users leave when debt-free (good for them, bad for business)

**Solutions:**
1. **Graduation program** - celebrate debt-free, ask for referrals
2. **Product expansion** - savings, investing, credit building
3. **Alumni community** - stay engaged with financial content
4. **Life events** - re-engage for mortgage, car buying
5. **Referral program** - best source of new users

## Trust & Safety

**Critical for success:**

1. **Security first**
- Bank-level encryption
- SOC 2 certification
- Read-only access (no money movement)

2. **Privacy**
- No selling data
- Anonymous analytics
- Clear data policy

3. **Accuracy**
- Strategy calculations verified by financial experts
- Clear disclaimers
- Not financial advice (educational tool)

4. **Transparency**
- Show how strategies work
- Explain all calculations
- Open about fees
