## Name

**RedlineRocketAI - AI Contract Review for High-Growth Companies**

---

## Core Concept

AI-powered contract review platform that redlines B2B agreements in minutes instead of hours. Trained on millions of contracts, the AI instantly identifies risky clauses (unlimited liability, one-sided termination, auto-renewal traps), benchmarks terms against industry standards, suggests redline language, negotiates counter-proposals based on company playbook, and integrates with e-signature workflows.

**The Bridge:** Legal Review ($500/hour) → [AI] → Automated Redline (100x faster, 100x cheaper)

## Problem Statement

### The Contract Review Bottleneck

- **Every B2B contract needs legal review** - Vendor agreements, MSAs, partnerships, employment offers
- **Outside counsel charges $300-600/hour** - High-growth companies sign hundreds of contracts annually
- **Each review takes 1-3 hours** - Even routine agreements require full review
- **Turnaround time is 24-72 hours** - Deals stall, teams wait, opportunities lost
- **Inconsistent quality** - Different lawyers flag different risks
