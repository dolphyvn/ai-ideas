# Revenue Model: PortDetentionAI

## Pricing Strategy

### Crisis Insurance Model (Primary Offering)
**Price:** $0/month monitoring + $25,000 one-time activation fee
**Target:** High-volume importers (50+ containers/month)
**Value Alignment:** Pay only when crisis occurs, aligned with customer success
**Features:**
- Unlimited shipment risk monitoring
- Predictive detention scoring
- OAI refusal pattern alerts
- 48-hour petition generation when activated
- 24/7 crisis support
- FDA liaison coordination
- Document extraction and preparation
- Petition submission and follow-through

**Economic Rationale:**
- Single detention event: $100K-1M cargo value lost
- Petition consultant: $10-20K, 7-21 day timeline (cargo often spoils first)
- Our pricing: 2x consultant cost, but 48-hour guarantee + ongoing monitoring
- Customer math: 1 prevented detention per year = 4-40x ROI

## Revenue Streams

### Primary: Crisis Insurance Activations (50% of revenue)
- **Model:** $0/month + $25K per detention event
- **Trigger:** When shipment detained and petition services activated
- **Frequency:** 2-5 activations per importer annually (average)
- **Margin:** 70% (high-touch delivery but one-time cost)

### Secondary: Risk Monitoring Subscriptions (30% of revenue)
- **Model:** Recurring SaaS subscription
- **Churn target:** <10% monthly (high switching costs)
- **Expansion path:** Importers upgrade tiers as volume grows

### Tertiary: Customs Broker Partnerships (15% of revenue)
- **Model:** Per-shipment API fees + referral commissions
- **Growth driver:** Brokers bundle offering with existing services
- **Integration value**: Embedded in broker workflows

### Quaternary: Manual Petitions (5% of revenue)
- **Model:** One-time fee for non-subscribers
- **Conversion purpose**: Upsell path to subscription
- **Higher margin**: 80% (no ongoing monitoring cost)

## Pricing Rationale

### Why $0/month + $25K Activation Works
1. **Psychological alignment**: "We win when you win" builds trust
2. **Risk reversal**: Importer pays nothing unless crisis occurs
3. **High willingness to pay**: $25K feels cheap vs $100K-1M loss
4. **Urgency conversion**: When detained, price insensitivity peaks
5. **Subscription objection overcome**: No wasted spend on "unused" service

### Why $499-4,999 Monitoring Tiers
1. **Value anchoring**: $500/month prevents 1 detention = 20x ROI
2. **Tiered adoption**: Start small, expand as value proven
3. **Budget alignment**: Fits logistics department budgets
4. **Competitive positioning**: 10x cheaper than hiring compliance staff

### Why $2 per Shipment for Brokers
1. **Economic alignment**: Broker charges $50-200 per shipment, $2 is 1-4%
2. **Volume incentives**: Discounts for high-volume brokers
3. **Embeddability**: Low enough to bundle without customer objection
4. **Revenue share**: 20% commission motivates broker promotion

### Why $5,000 Manual Petions
1. **Competitive positioning**: 50% cheaper than consultants ($10-20K)
2. **Speed premium**: 48 hours vs 7-21 days
3. **Conversion tool**: Importers experience value, convert to subscription
4. **Margin attractive**: 80% margin funds customer acquisition

## Pricing Iteration Strategy

### Phase 1: Penetration Pricing (Months 1-12)
- Crisis insurance at $25K (test willingness to pay)
- Monitoring tiers priced to acquire customers
- Focus on speed to value, not margin maximization

### Phase 2: Value Pricing (Months 13-24)
- Increase crisis insurance to $35K if conversion >60%

- Add premium tier ($50K) for 24-hour petition turnaround

### Phase 3: Value-Based Pricing (Year 3+)

- Higher prices for high-risk product-origin corridors
- Custom enterprise pricing with outcome-based guarantees
