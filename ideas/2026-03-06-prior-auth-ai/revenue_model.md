# Revenue Model: Prior Auth AI

## Pricing Strategy

**Price:** $799/month/provider

**Target:**
- Practices with 1-5 providers
- Single specialty focus
- Single or dual payer
- High prior auth volume but limited complexity

**Features:**
- Up to 5 providers per practice
- Single specialty (cardiology, oncology, etc.)
- Single payer (Medicare or one commercial)
- Basic EHR integration (chart export)
- Long-context chart comprehension (200K tokens)
- Payer rule database (single payer)
- Clinical justification generation
- Submission package compilation
- Email support

**Example:** 3-provider cardiology practice = $2,397/month

## Pricing Rationale

### Why Practices Will Pay $799-2,499/month/provider

**1. Staff Replacement Economics**
- Cost of prior auth specialist: $65,000 salary + $13,000 benefits + $8,000 overhead = $86,000/year
- Platform cost: $799-2,499 × 12 = $9,588-29,988/year
- Net savings: $56,000-76,000/year per FTE eliminated

**2. Approval Rate Improvement**
- Industry average first-approval: 60-70%
- Prior Auth AI target: 85%+
- Denial rework cost: $250+ per denial
- For 200 prior auths/month: 20-40 fewer denials = $5,000-10,000/month savings

**3. Time-to-Treatment Improvement**
- Manual prior auth: 2-5 days
- Prior Auth AI: 3-4 hours
- Patient satisfaction and retention value: Difficult to quantify but significant

**4. Staff Turnover Elimination**
- Industry turnover: 35% annually
- Cost of turnover: $15,000 per replacement (hiring + training)
- Platform eliminates this cost entirely

**5. Scalability**
- Human staff: Linear scaling (more cases = more hires)
- AI platform: Fixed cost (unlimited cases)

### Price Elasticity Analysis

**Inelastic Demand Factors:**
- High urgency (patient harm)
- Clear ROI calculation
- Staff elimination is permanent
- Switching costs are very high

**Elastic Demand Factors:**
- Practice budget constraints
- Competitor pricing (if emerges)
- Alternative solutions (outsourcing companies)

**Optimal Price Point:**
- 10-20% of displaced staff cost
- Practices see 3-8x ROI
- Low enough for rapid adoption
- High enough for premium positioning

## Pricing Iteration Strategy

### Phase 1: Penetration Pricing (Months 1-12)

- Lower initial pricing to drive adoption
- Focus on cardiology-only, Medicare-only
- $499/month/provider for early adopters
- Grandfathered pricing for first 100 practices

### Phase 2: Value-Based Pricing (Months 13-36)

- Increase pricing based on proven ROI
- Introduce tiered pricing (Starter, Professional, Enterprise)
- $799-2,499/month/provider based on features
- Annual contracts with quarterly billing

### Phase 3: Outcome-Based Pricing (Month 36+)

- Optional outcome-based pricing
- Base fee + success fee on approval rate improvement
- Example: $500/month/provider + $50 per approved prior auth
- Aligns incentives with practice success

### Geographic Pricing

- No geographic variation (national pricing)
- Simplifies sales, reduces pricing friction
- All payers have same rules nationwide

### Contract Length Discounts

- Monthly: No discount (month-to-month)
- Annual: 10% discount (standard)
- 3-Year: 20% discount (enterprise only)
- Prepayment: Additional 5% discount
