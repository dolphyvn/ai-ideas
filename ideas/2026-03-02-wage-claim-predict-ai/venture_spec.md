**Date:** 2026-03-02

## Core Concept

Predictive wage claim risk platform using proprietary settlement and DOL investigation data. Analyzes employer payroll practices, classifies risk profiles, predicts claim likelihood, and recommends preventive actions before lawsuits are filed.

**The Bridge:** Reactive Claim Defense (after lawsuit filed) → [AI] → Predictive Risk Modeling (prevent claims before filed)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Wage Claim Epidemic

- **$1.1B in back wages recovered** by DOL Wage and Hour Division annually
- **10,000+ FLSA lawsuits filed** each year
- **Average settlement: $50K-500K** for mid-sized employers
- **Employers have no visibility** into claim risk until served
- **Insurance underwriting blind** - no data to price EPLI accurately

### The Current Reality

standard. We'd pay $500/month for AI that actually predicted

Now we self-insure EPLI and need better risk intelligence."

## Why Now

### 1. DOL Enforcement Surge

- DOL Wage and Hour Division budget up 40% since 2021
- Joint DOJ-IRS worker classification task force
- State AGs aggressively pursuing wage claims

### 2. EPLI Market Inefficiency

- Employment Practices Liability Insurance premiums up 30% since 2020
- Underwriters lack data - price based on industry heuristics
- Claims prevention = lower premiums (win-win)

### 3. Litigation Funding Explosion

- Third-party litigation finance funds wage class actions
- Plaintiff lawyers on aggressive outreach
- Social media amplifies wage theft allegations

### 4. No Predictive Tools Exist

- All tools are reactive (audit prep after notice)
- No one has proprietary claim outcome data
- Employers flying blind

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- Risk scoring (payroll practice analysis vs. claim patterns)
- Predictive modeling (ML on 10,000+ claim outcomes)
- Prevention recommendations (specific policy/process fixes)
- Benchmarking (anonymous employer comparisons)
- EPLI underwriting support (data for insurance pricing)

**Defensibility:**
- **Proprietary claim data** - Settlement/outcome data NOT publicly available
- **Data network effects** - More employers = better predictions
- **ML model moat** - Predictive accuracy = competitive advantage
