# Denial Prevention AI - Revenue Model

## Pricing

**Tier 1: Solo Provider** - $500/month
- Up to 500 claims/month
- Core denial prevention
- Email reports

**Tier 2: Small Practice (2-5 providers)** - $1,500/month
- Up to 2,500 claims/month
- Priority support
- Custom payer rules

**Tier 3: Group Practice (6-20 providers)** - $5,000/month
- Unlimited claims
- Dedicated success manager
- Quarterly review

**Enterprise:** Custom pricing for hospitals/health systems
