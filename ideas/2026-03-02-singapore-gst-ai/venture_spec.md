## Name

## Core Concept

AI-powered platform for Singapore tax practitioners that automates GST compliance, validates InvoiceNow mandate compliance (November 2025), detects pre-audit anomalies using ML (mirroring IRAS's own AI audit methods), and prevents IRAS inquiries. Focuses on Singapore-specific complexity including GST 9%, transfer pricing documentation, cross-border DTA claims, and 5-year record retention requirements.

---

## Problem Statement

### The Regulatory Burden

- **15,000+ ISCA members** practicing in Singapore
- **~500,000+ GST-registered businesses** (threshold: S$1M annual turnover)
- **InvoiceNow mandate effective November 2025** - ALL invoice issuers must comply
- **IRAS penalty regime** - Up to 200% of GST + S$5,000 fines + jail time
- **IRAS AI-driven audits** - >3% variance triggers automatic review

### The InvoiceNow Mandate

```
InvoiceNow Requirements (Effective November 2025):
- All invoices must include UEN identification
- GST invoices must meet specific format requirements
- e-Invoicing via Peppol network encouraged
- 5-year record retention (digital format acceptable)
- Real-time IRAS data matching capability

Non-compliance penalties:
- Late filing: 5% per month (max 50%)
- Incorrect GST: 200% of underpaid amount + S$5,000
- Record-keeping failures: Up to S$5,000
```

### The IRAS Audit Reality

| IRAS AI Audit Trigger | What Happens | Impact |
|----------------------|--------------|--------|
| **>3% GST variance** | Auto-inquiry generated | 4-6 week disruption |
| **Missing DTA docs** | Full transfer pricing audit | 12+ month investigation |
| **InvoiceNow format error** | Penalty + rejection | Compliance failure |
| **Cross-border mismatch** | GST + corporate tax audit | Multi-tax review |

### The Tax Practitioner Experience

```
"IRAS sent an inquiry letter - our client's GST filings showed

documents, responding to queries, and reconciling transactions.
```
