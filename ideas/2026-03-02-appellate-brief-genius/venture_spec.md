# Appellate Brief Genius - AI-Powered Appellate Brief Drafting

## Core Concept

AI-powered appellate brief drafting platform that ingests trial court records, identifies appealable issues using error preservation analysis, and drafts brief sections with precedent citations from appellate opinion databases.

**The Bridge:** Manual Appellate Review → [AI] → Automated Brief Drafting (error detection, issue identification, precedent citation)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Appellate Brief Bottleneck

- **8,000+ appellate attorneys** in the US
- **Appellate briefs require 40-80 hours** of preparation
- **Appellate billing $500-800/hr** - brief value $20K-60K+
- **Missed errors = lost appeal** - irreversible malpractice exposure
- **Error preservation analysis** requires 100+ hours of transcript review

### The Current Reality

```
"We spent 120 hours reviewing a 2,000-page trial transcript for
preserved error, then another 60 hours drafting the issue statement
and argument sections. We missed a critical preservation issue that
the appellate court flagged. We'd pay $3,000 per appeal for AI
that actually catches all appealable issues and drafts with proper
precedent citations."
- Appellate Partner, Boutique Firm
```

## Why Now

### 1. Appellate Stakes Are Extreme

- Briefs determine case outcomes
- Missed errors = lost appeal (irreversible)
- Malpractice exposure is significant

### 2. Public Appellate Data

- Appellate opinions are free (CourtListener, Google Scholar)
- PACER trial transcripts accessible (expensive but structured)
- Error preservation patterns well-documented in precedent

### 3. LLMs Excel at Brief Drafting

- Legal document synthesis is LLM-native
- Citation generation is established capability
- Case similarity matching works well

### 4. Underserved Niche

- Appellate practice is small but high-value
- Enterprise tools focus on trial litigation
- Boutique firms lack specialized tools
