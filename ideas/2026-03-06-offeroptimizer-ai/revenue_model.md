# OfferOptimizer Revenue Model

**B2C Economics, Viral Loops, and Unit Economics**

---

## Revenue Model Overview

### Core Philosophy

> "Pay when you get an offer, not a subscription you'll forget."

OfferOptimizer's revenue model is built on three principles:

1. **Trigger-Based Pricing** - Pay when valuable (got an offer), not monthly
2. **Clear ROI** - User gets $10K+ raise, pays $49 (0.5% of value)
3. **LinkedIn Viral Amplification** - Success stories are inherently shareable

## Viral Loop Economics

### K-Factor Calculation

```
K = (Conversions per Share) × (Share Rate) × (Conversion Rate)

Baseline:
- Conversions per Share: 10% (LinkedIn engagement)
- Share Rate: 20% (successful users post)
- Conversion Rate: 15% (viewers analyze offers)

K = 0.10 × 0.20 × 0.15 = 0.003 (low - LinkedIn less viral than IG)

With Optimization:
- Conversions per Share: 15% (better targeting)
- Share Rate: 40% (incentivized sharing)
- Conversion Rate: 25% (stronger social proof)

K = 0.15 × 0.40 × 0.25 = 0.015

Target: K = 0.05-0.10 (LinkedIn is more professional, less viral)
```

**Note:** Lower K-factor is acceptable due to:
- Higher ARPU ($49 vs $79/year)
- Instant payment (no churn risk)
- Professional audience quality

**Churn by Plan:**

| Plan | Churn Type | Rate | Mitigation |

**Churn is Different:**

- Goal: Win back when next offer comes
- Strategy: "Career tracker" - stay top of mind

### Win-Back Campaigns

**Trigger: Job change detected**

```

- LinkedIn update (new position)

Win-back offer:
"Congrats on the new role! When you're ready for your next
negotiation, come back for 40% off. Here's a code..."
```

**Target Win-Back Rate:** 30-40% (warm leads)

## Pricing Experiments

### A/B Test Framework

**Always Be Testing:**

1. **Price Point Testing**
   - $39 vs $49 vs $59 (single)
   - $79 vs $99 vs $119 (bundle)
   - $149 vs $199 vs $249 (unlimited)

2. **Offer Testing**
   - Guarantee: "No raise, money back" vs "Best scripts, guaranteed"
   - Trial: "Free script, pay for full package" vs "Pay upfront"
   - Bundling: Include resume review vs standalone

3. **Psychological Triggers**
   - Scarcity: "Prices increasing next month"
   - Social Proof: "Join 10,000 who negotiated successfully"
   - Loss Aversion: "Leaving $20K on the table costs $49 to fix"

### Dynamic Pricing (Future)

## Economic Moats: Revenue Protection

### Pricing Power

**Why competitors can't easily undercut:**

1. **Outcome Data Moat** - Better negotiation data = better results = higher value
2. **Salary Data Moat** - Proprietary database not easily replicated
3. **Brand Equity** - First mover advantage in "AI negotiation"
4. **Network Effects** - More users = better salary data = better product

### Pricing Defense Strategy

**If competitor undercuts by 50%:**

1. **Value Communication**
   - Success rate superiority
   - Real outcomes, not just market data
   - Roleplay practice (unique feature)

2. **Feature Differentiation**
   - Company-specific intelligence
   - Offer comparison tools
   - Career tracking (sticky feature)

3. **Outcome Guarantees**
   - "No raise, full refund"
   - "Beat any competing offer"
   - Success stories and testimonials
