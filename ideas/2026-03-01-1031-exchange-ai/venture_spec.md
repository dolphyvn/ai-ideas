# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**1031AI - Like-Kind Exchange Platform**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered platform for Qualified Intermediaries (QIs) that automates 1031 exchange facilitation, property identification tracking, timing compliance, and IRS reporting. Handles the critical 45-day identification and 180-day closing deadlines.

## Problem:
- **3,000+ Qualified Intermediaries** in US
- **$100B+ annual 1031 exchange volume** (real estate tax deferral)
- **45-day identification deadline**: Miss = disqualified, capital gains tax due
- **180-day closing deadline**: Miss = disqualified
- **Multi-property identification**: 3-property rule, 200% rule, 95% rule
- **Like-kind determination**: Complex IRS rules (Publication 1031)
- **QI compliance**: Funds must be held by QI, constructive receipt avoidance

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** Qualified Intermediaries (QIs)
- Independent QIs (solo practitioners)
- QI firms (10-50 QIs)
- Title companies with QI divisions
- Real estate attorneys offering 1031 services

**Secondary:** Real estate investors, commercial real estate brokers, tax attorneys

## Why Now:
1. **High interest rates**: Investors holding properties longer = 1031 more valuable
2. **Real estate appreciation**: $100K-$1M+ capital gains at stake
3. **1031 attack risk**: Congress periodically proposes eliminating 1031 (creates urgency)
4. **Crypto 1031 emerging**: IRS issued guidance (2023) on crypto 1031
5. **QI consolidation**: Title companies buying QIs, mid-market underserved

## AI Advantage:
- **Timing compliance**: Auto-tracks 45/180-day deadlines, sends alerts
- **Property identification**: AI analyzes like-kind eligibility (real property tests)
- **Multi-property optimization**: 3-property vs. 200% vs. 95% rule analysis
- **Constructive receipt prevention**: AI flags risky QI actions
- **IRS form preparation**: 8824 (Like-Kind Exchanges) auto-generation
- **Crypto 1031**: Bitcoin/ETH like-kind determination (emerging area)

## Viral Mechanism:
- **FETIA**: Federation of Exchange Accommodators (QI trade association)
- **Real estate investor networks**: "Never miss 45-day deadline" = compelling
- **Commercial brokers**: High-value deals = 1031 critical
- **Case studies**: "Saved $500K capital gains using AI"
- **Tax attorney referrals**: Real estate tax attorneys recommend

## Revenue Model:
**Per-QI Subscription:**
- **Solo**: $400/month - Single QI, up to 20 exchanges/year
- **Firm**: $1,200/month - Up to 10 QIs, unlimited exchanges
- **Enterprise**: $3,000/month - Unlimited + crypto 1031 + white-label + API

**Per-Exchange add-on:**
- $100 per 1031 exchange (beyond included tiers)

**Implementation:**
- $4,000 one-time (exchange migration, deadline tracking setup, training)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $150 (FETIA conferences, real estate partnerships)
- LTV: $21,600 (36-month retention - switching costs high)
- Gross Margin: 83%

## Moat:
1. **QI licensing**: No formal license but professional standard, FETIA membership
2. **IRS 1031 rules**: Complex timing (45/180 days), like-kind tests
3. **Funds handling**: QI must hold funds (escrow), constructive receipt avoidance
4. **Timing criticality**: Deadlines are unforgiving (IRS)
5. **Real estate expertise**: Property identification, valuation, closing coordination

## MVP in 12 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-3:**
- Focus: Timing tracking only (45/180-day deadlines)
- Manual property identification workflow
- One QI pilot

**Weeks 4-6:**

- Property identification analyzer (like-kind eligibility)

**Weeks 7-9:**
- Partner with 3 QIs (solo, mid-size, large)
- Deploy pilot for active 1031 exchanges

**Weeks 10-12:**
- Add multi-property rule analysis (3-property, 200%, 95%)

**Validation Metrics:**

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: GPT-4 (IRS Publication 1031 analysis), Custom ML (like-kind classification)
- **Integrations**: County recorders (property verification), IRS e-filing
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (US regions, IRS compliance)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $20K MRR (50 QIs × $400 ARPU)
- **Year 2**: $100K MRR (250 QIs + enterprise)
- **Year 3**: $300K MRR (750 QIs + crypto 1031)

## Risk Factors:
1. **Congressional elimination**: 1031 could be eliminated (periodic threat)
2. **Interest rates**: Lower rates = less real estate holding = fewer 1031s
3. **QI consolidation**: Title companies buying QIs
4. **Crypto 1033 uncertainty**: IRS may change crypto treatment (1033 vs. 1031)
5. **Liability risk**: Wrong advice = capital gains tax due for client

## Competitive Threat:
- **Exchange companies**: Service businesses, not software
- **Title companies**: Have QI divisions but not software-focused
- **Real estate software**: CRM/deal management, not 1031-specific
- **DIY 1031 services**: Limited automation

**Differentiation**: Only AI platform for 1031 exchanges with deadline tracking, like-kind analysis, multi-property optimization, and crypto 1031 support.
