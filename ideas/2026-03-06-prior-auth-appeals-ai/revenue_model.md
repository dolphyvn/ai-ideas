# Prior Auth Appeals AI - Revenue Model

## Pricing

**Tier 1: Solo Provider** - $750/month
- Up to 25 appeals/month
- Core appeal generation
- Email support

**Tier 2: Small Practice (2-5 providers)** - $2,000/month
- Up to 100 appeals/month
- Peer-to-peer prep materials
- Priority support

**Tier 3: Group Practice (6-20 providers)** - $6,000/month
- Unlimited appeals
- IRO packet generation
- Dedicated success manager

**Bundled with Cycle 110:** $1,000/month (vs $1,250 separately)
- 20% discount for both products

## Unit Economics

**CAC (New Customer):** $2,000
- Higher touch sales (clinical credibility required)

**CAC (Upsell from Cycle 110):** $200
- Email nurture, demo, existing trust

**Blended CAC:** $800
- 30% new, 70% upsell

**LTV:** $27,000
- $750/month × 36 month avg lifetime
- Bundle customers: $36,000 LTV

**LTV:CAC:** 34x

**Payback Period:** 1 month (upsell), 3 months (new)
