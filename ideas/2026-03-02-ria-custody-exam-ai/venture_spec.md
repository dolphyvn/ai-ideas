## Core Concept

AI-powered custody rule compliance platform for RIAs. Automates SEC Rule 206(4)-2 compliance including surprise exam scheduling, qualified custodian verification, written agreement documentation, and ledger reconciliation. Uses ML to detect custody violation patterns from SEC enforcement data.

**The Bridge:** Manual Custody Compliance (Excel, missed surprise exams, violations) → [AI] → Automated Custody Monitoring (exam-ready, zero violations)
