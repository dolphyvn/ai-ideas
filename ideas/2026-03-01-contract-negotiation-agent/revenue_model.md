# Revenue Model - Contract Negotiation Agent

## Pricing Strategy

### Tiered Subscription

| Tier | Price | Features | Target |
|------|-------|----------|--------|
| Starter | $499/mo | 10 contracts/mo, standard playbooks, email support | Startups, small companies |
| Growth | $1,999/mo | Unlimited contracts, custom playbooks, priority support | Mid-market ($10M-$100M ARR) |
| Enterprise | $4,999/mo + 10% savings | Dedicated playbooks, SLA, API access, account manager | Large enterprises |

### Implementation Fees
- $2,500 one-time for Enterprise (playbook customization, integration)
- $0 for Starter/Growth (self-service)

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Content marketing**: LinkedIn posts, case studies = $200
- **Cold outreach**: Targeted CFO emails = $150
- **Referrals**: 20% of new customers = $0
- **Sales calls**: For Enterprise only = $500

**Blended CAC: ~$500**

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Avg ARR**: $18,000 (Growth tier avg)
- **Gross margin**: 85%
- **Retention**: 24 months avg
- **Expansion**: 20% annual (tier upgrades)

**LTV: $18,000 × 24 × 1.2 = $51,840**
**LTV (gross margin): $44,064**

### Payback Period
- CAC: $500
- Monthly net revenue: $1,275 ($1,500 × 85%)
- **Payback: <4 months**
