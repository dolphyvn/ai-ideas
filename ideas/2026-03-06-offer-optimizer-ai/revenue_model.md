# Revenue Model: OfferOptimizer

## Core Value Proposition
$49 to make $5,000-$20,000 more in salary. Clear ROI.

## Pricing Strategy

### Consumer Model: Pay-Per-Offer

**Tier 1: Free - Market Research**
- Salary benchmarking against market data
- Basic offer comparison
- No scripts, no coaching
- Purpose: Lead generation and data collection

**Tier 2: Single Offer - $49**
- Full offer analysis (salary, equity, benefits)
- Custom negotiation scripts (email + phone)
- Market benchmarking
- Counter-offer simulations
- One-time use, expires after 30 days

**Tier 3: Job Search Bundle - $99**
- Everything in Single Offer
- Unlimited offers for 3 months
- Voice practice sessions (5 included)
- Equity/RSU modeling
- Ongoing market data updates

**Tier 4: Voice Practice Add-on - $20**
- Unlimited AI negotiation conversations
- Real-time feedback on delivery
- Objection handling practice
- Confidence scoring

### Alternative: Success Fee Model

**Success Fee Option - 10% of additional salary (capped at $2,000)**
- No upfront cost
- Pay only if negotiation successful
- Verification required (offer letter comparison)
- Higher effective price for large gains

**Why offer both:** Risk-averse users prefer success fee, confident users prefer flat fee.

## B2B2C Model: Enterprise Partnerships

**University Career Centers - $10K/year**
- Site license for all students
- Co-branded landing pages
- Usage analytics (which majors negotiate most)
- Success metrics for career center reporting
- Target: Large universities (100+ institutions)

**Outplacement Firms - $25K/year**
- For companies doing layoffs
- White-label solution for displaced workers
- Bulk pricing: $5/employee
- Success metrics for HR reporting
- Target: Major outplacement firms

**Recruiting Agencies - $15K/year**
- For candidates they place
- Value-add to attract candidates
- Placement success rate improvement
- Target: Mid-sized agencies

## Unit Economics

### Consumer Pay-Per-Offer

**Acquisition (CAC):**
- LinkedIn content: $30
- Reddit/communities: $15
- Referrals: $10
- **Blended CAC: $60**

**Revenue (ARPU):**
- Free users: $0 (30%)
- Single offer: $49 (40%)
- Job search bundle: $99 (25%)
- Success fee: varies (5%)
- **Blended ARPU: $55**

**Retention & LTV:**
- Avg offers per job search: 2.5
- Job changes every 3 years
- **LTV: $55 × 2.5 = $137**

**Gross Margin:**
- API costs: $5 per analysis
- Support: $2 per user
- **COGS: $7 (12%)**
- **Gross Margin: 88%**

**Payback Period:**
- CAC: $60
- Gross profit per user: $48
- Payback: 1.3 customers (~1 month)

### B2B2C Partnerships

**Sales Cycle:** 3-6 months
**Deal Size:** $10K-$50K/year
**Churn:** <10% (contract-based)
**Gross Margin:** 70% (account management costs)

## Churn Mitigation

**Problem:** Users leave after landing job

**Solutions:**
1. **Career-long tools**: Internal offer reviews, promotion negotiations
2. **Network effects**: Refer friends for free months
3. **Annual plans**: Lock in for 12 months
4. **Expanded services**: Resume, interview prep, career coaching
5. **Alumni community**: Keep users engaged with career content
