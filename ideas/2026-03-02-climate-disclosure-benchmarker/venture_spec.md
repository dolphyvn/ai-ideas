## Core Concept

AI-powered platform that analyzes SEC climate disclosures and generates competitive benchmarking reports. The system uses LLMs to extract emissions data, climate risk scenarios, and governance practices from thousands of SEC filings, allowing companies to compare their disclosures against peers and industry leaders.

**The Bridge:** Manual Benchmarking (expensive ESG data providers, manual analysis) → [AI] → Automated Disclosure Analysis (public database, peer comparison, gap analysis)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Climate Disclosure Benchmarking Gap

- **5,000+ US public companies** now required to disclose (SEC + California)
- **Companies fear under-disclosing** relative to peers (investor scrutiny)
- **ESG data providers charge $25K-50K+** for benchmarking reports
- **Manual analysis** of competitor filings is time-consuming
- **Quality varies widely** - inconsistent disclosures make comparison difficult

### The Current Reality

```
"Our sustainability team spends weeks manually reviewing competitor
SEC filings to understand our climate disclosure positioning. We pay
MSCI $40K annually for benchmarking data, but it's mostly numbers.
We can't tell if our climate risk scenarios are comparable to peers.
We'd pay $15K for AI that actually extracts and compares qualitative
disclosures like risk scenarios and governance practices."
- VP Sustainability, Mid-Market Public Company
```

## Why Now

### 1. Regulatory Expansion

- SEC climate disclosure rules (even with legal challenges, disclosures continue)
- California SB 253/261 ensures market exists
- EU CSRD creates international comparability

### 2. Investor Pressure

- Asset managers requiring consistent ESG data
- Climate risk as fiduciary duty consideration
- TCFT alignment becoming standard

### 3. Public SEC Data Available

- All SEC filings on EDGAR (complete dataset)
- Climate disclosures increasing exponentially
- No proprietary integration required

### 4. ESG Data Provider Gap

- MSCI, Sustainalytics focus on quantitative scores
- Qualitative analysis (risk scenarios, governance) underdeveloped
- High pricing excludes mid-market

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- SEC filing extraction (OCR + LLM for 10-K climate sections)
- Emissions data normalization (Scope 1-2-3 comparability)
- Risk scenario analysis (TCFD scenario comparison)
- Governance practice extraction (board oversight, incentive alignment)
- Benchmarking reports (industry, peer, regional comparisons)

**Defensibility:**
