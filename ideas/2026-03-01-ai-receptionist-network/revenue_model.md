# Revenue Model - AI Receptionist Network

## Pricing Strategy

### Per-Location Subscription

| Tier | Price | Minutes | Features | Target |
|------|-------|---------|----------|--------|
| Starter | $99/mo | 100 | Standard voice, basic integrations | Solo practitioners |
| Pro | $199/mo | 500 | Custom voice clone, priority support | Busy salons, shops |
| Enterprise | $399/mo | Unlimited | Multi-location, API, dedicated rep | Multi-location businesses |

### Setup Fees
- $149 one-time (voice training, calendar integration)
- Waived for annual commitments

---

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Walk-ins**: $15 (time only, local visits)
- **Referrals**: $0 (20% of new customers)
- **Local ads (Google Local)**: $30
- **Word-of-mouth**: $0

**Blended CAC: ~$35**

### Lifetime Value (LTV)
- **Avg ARPU**: $150/mo (mix of tiers)
- **Gross margin**: 65% (voice API costs are real)
- **Retention**: 18 months avg (SMB churn is high)
- **Expansion**: 15% (tier upgrades, multi-location)

**LTV: $150 × 18 × 0.65 × 1.15 = $2,022**
**LTV (gross margin): $1,314**

### Payback Period
- CAC: $35
- Monthly net revenue: $97 ($150 × 65%)
- **Payback: <2 months**

## Cost Structure

### Variable Costs (per customer)
- **Voice API (Vapi/Retell)**: $35/month (avg 150 minutes)
- **Phone number (Twilio)**: $5/month
- **Voice cloning (ElevenLabs)**: $10/month
- **Hosting/infrastructure**: $2/month

**Total COGS: ~$52/month (35% of revenue = 65% margin)**

### Fixed Costs (Year 1)
- **Engineering**: 2 FTE × $120K = $240,000
- **Sales (local)**: 2 FTE × $60K + commission = $150,000
- **Support**: 1 FTE × $50K = $50,000
- **Tools/infrastructure**: $30,000

**Total Fixed: ~$470,000**

## Churn Mitigation

### Why SMBs Churn
- "Business is slow" (temporary)
- "Too complicated" (usability)
- "Not enough calls" (perceived value)

### Retention Strategies
- **Pause not cancel**: $10/month to keep number
- **Success reporting**: "You got 15 calls this month"
- **Usage-based pricing**: Pay for what you use
- **Annual discounts**: 20% off for 12-month commitment
