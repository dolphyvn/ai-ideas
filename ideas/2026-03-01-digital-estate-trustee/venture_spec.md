# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**AI Trustee for Digital Estate Settlement**

## Core Concept:
Court-approved AI trustee for automated estate administration with digital asset distribution and probate compliance. Handles the $84T wealth transfer by managing digital estates (crypto, NFTs, domain names, social media accounts) through court-supervised processes.

## Problem:
- **$84T wealth transfer crisis**: 2024-2045 intergenerational wealth transfer overwhelming courts
- **Digital asset chaos**: Crypto wallets lost, NFTs inaccessible, digital accounts frozen
- **33% have wills**: Even fewer address digital assets
- **Court backlog**: Probate cases taking 12-24 months
- **Crypto inheritance lawsuits**: Surge in high-profile disputes

## Target Vertical:
**Primary**: Probate courts and estate law firms
- State probate courts (overwhelmed with digital asset cases)
- Estate planning attorneys (can't handle volume)
- Trustees/executors (need digital asset expertise)

**Secondary**: Crypto-native individuals, digital asset holders

## Why Now:
1. **NAR settlement wake-up**: Estate planning urgency increased
2. **Crypto maturation**: Early adopters dying with significant digital estates
3. **Court innovation**: Some courts piloting digital systems
4. **Legal precedent**: Digital asset inheritance cases establishing frameworks
5. **$84T transfer**: Already underway, creating immediate crisis

## AI Advantage:
- **Asset discovery**: Finds digital assets across blockchains, platforms
- **Legal compliance**: Navigates state probate laws automatically
- **Beneficiary communication**: Handles court-required notifications
- **Accounting**: Real-time ledger of estate transactions
- **Court filing**: Generates required probate documents

## Viral Mechanism:
- **High-profile cases**: Celebrity crypto estate disputes drive awareness
- **Legal community**: Estate attorneys refer within networks
- **Court adoption**: One court approves → others follow
- **Crypto media**: Bitcoin/eth communities cover digital estate issues

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Estate Fee:**
- **Standard**: $2,500 + 1% of estate value - Digital estates under $1M
- **Complex**: $7,500 + 0.5% - Crypto-heavy, multi-jurisdictional
- **Enterprise**: Subscription for courts - $25K/year

**Court Partnership:**
- Revenue share with jurisdictions

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $1,000 (legal conferences, court outreach)
- LTV: $8,000 (avg estate)
- Gross Margin: 75%

## Moat:
1. **Court approval**: Once approved, hard to displace
2. **Fiduciary bonds**: Required bonding creates barrier
3. **State bar relationships**: Trust is gatekept
4. **Blockchain expertise**: Private key management, legal transfer
5. **Precedent database**: Case law across jurisdictions

## MVP in 7 Days:
**Days 1-2:**
- Manual digital asset discovery
- One blockchain (Ethereum)
- Template probate documents

**Days 3-4:**
- Asset tracing tools (blockchain APIs)
- Probate document generation
- Beneficiary notification templates

**Days 5-7:**
- Partner with 1 estate attorney
- Process 1 real probate case
- Get court filing accepted

**Validation Metric:**
- 1 court accepts AI trustee filing
- 1 estate attorney willing to partner
- Digital assets successfully transferred

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **Blockchain**: Web3.py, Etherscan APIs
- **Document**: Court filing templates
- **Security**: Multi-party computation for key management
- **Database**: PostgreSQL (estate records)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $40K MRR (20 estates/month × $2K avg)
- **Year 2**: $150K MRR (75 estates/month)
- **Year 3**: $400K MRR (200 estates + court partnerships)

## Risk Factors:
1. **Court acceptance**: Judges may reject AI trustees
2. **Liability**: Wrong asset transfer = legal exposure
3. **Regulatory uncertainty**: Digital asset laws evolving
4. **Key management**: Security risk for private keys
5. **State variation**: 50 different probate systems

## Competitive Threat:
- Traditional trust companies (slow, not crypto-native)
- Estate attorneys (manual, can't scale)
- Crypto inheritance platforms (non-court solutions)

**Differentiation**: Court-approved, legally binding, full probate compliance
