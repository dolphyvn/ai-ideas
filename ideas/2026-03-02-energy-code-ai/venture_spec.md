## Core Concept

AI-powered energy code compliance checking for residential and commercial projects. Automates IECC 2024, ASHRAE 90.1-2023, and California Title 24 compliance calculations. Generates HERS index predictions, COMcheck reports, and Title 24 documentation from architectural drawings and specifications.

**The Bridge:** Manual Energy Calculations (expensive consultants, slow, error-prone) → [AI] → Automated Compliance Reports (minutes, permit-ready)

- **30+ states adopting 2024 IECC** with massive complexity increase
- **Cannot get permit without energy compliance** - permit-blocking requirement
- **HERS ratings cost $500-2,000 per project** - expensive and slow

- **Energy consultant shortage** - 4-6 week wait times common

## Why Now

### 1. 2024 IECC Adoption Wave

- 30+ states adopting updated energy code in 2024-2025
- Code complexity increased 40% vs. 2018 version
- Many architects lack energy code expertise

### 2. California Title 24 Strictest

- Title 24 is most stringent energy code in US
- California = 40% of US construction market
- Non-compliance = permit rejection

### 3. Climate Policy Pressure

- Cities adopting building performance standards (Boston, NYC, DC)
- Electrification mandates creating new compliance requirements
- Net-zero carbon targets driving complexity

### 4. HERS Rater Shortage

- Certified HERS raters cannot meet demand
- 4-6 week wait times common
- Turnkey solution sells at premium

## AI Advantage

**Core Capabilities:**
- Energy modeling from drawings (square footage, glazing, insulation values)
- IECC compliance checking (prescriptive and performance paths)
- Title 24 calculation generation (CF-1R, CF-2R forms)
- HERS index prediction (target setting for raters)
- COMcheck report automation (commercial projects)
- Ashrae 90.1 compliance (large commercial)

**Defensibility:**
- **Regulatory complexity** - 50 states + hundreds of cities with unique codes
- **Calculation complexity** - RESCHECK, COMcheck, Title 24 genuinely difficult
- **Accumulated project data** - learn what passes in each jurisdiction
- **HERS rater channel** - 30% revenue share creates lock-in
