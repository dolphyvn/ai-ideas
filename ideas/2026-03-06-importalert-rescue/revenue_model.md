# Revenue Model: ImportAlertRescue.ai

## Pricing Strategy

### Crisis Insurance Model (Primary Offering - Preventive)
**Price:** $0/month monitoring + $20,000 one-time activation fee
**Target:** OAI-risk companies (not yet on Import Alert)
**Value Alignment:** Pay only when crisis occurs, aligned with customer success
**Features:**
- Unlimited manufacturer/origin monitoring
- OAI-to-Alert risk prediction (6-month window)
- Automated petition preparation if alert occurs
- 5-shipment clean tracking
- FDA response coordination
- 24/7 crisis support

**Economic Rationale:**
- Single Import Alert month: $50K-500K in blocked shipments
- Manual removal: $50-200K attorney fees, 12-24 months
- Our pricing: 40% of attorney cost, 4-month target timeline
- Customer math: Prevent 1 month of alert = 2-25x ROI

### Active Alert Removal (High-Intent Offering)
**Price:** $50,000 flat fee for complete removal service
**Target:** Companies currently on FDA Import Alert
**Payment Structure:** 50% upfront, 50% upon petition submission
**Success Guarantee:** Petition rejected = 50% refund
**Features:**
- Complete removal petition (AI-generated, attorney-reviewed)
- Evidence document extraction and organization
- 5-clean-shipment monitoring and verification
- FDA response coordination
- Weekly progress updates
- Target 4-month removal timeline

**Economic Rationale:**
- Monthly alert cost: $50K-500K in lost revenue
- Attorney alternative: $50-200K, 12-24 month timeline
- Our pricing: Competitive mid-point, faster delivery
- Customer math: 4-month removal vs 18-month = 14 months × $100K = $1.4M savings

### Tier 1: High-Risk Monitoring (OAI Companies)
**Price:** $799/month or $7,990/year (2 months free)
**Target:** Companies with recent OAI designation (elevated risk)
**Features:**
- Up to 10 manufacturers/origins monitored
- OAI-to-Alert risk scoring
- Predictive alerts (6-month window)
- Monthly risk reports
- Remediation roadmap recommendations
- Email support

### Tier 2: Multi-Source Monitoring
**Price:** $2,499/month or $24,990/year (2 months free)
**Target:** Importers with 10+ manufacturers or multiple origins
**Features:**
- Unlimited manufacturers/origins monitored
- Everything in Tier 1
- API access for integration
- Real-time alert notifications
- White-label broker portal access
- Priority support
- Quarterly compliance review

### Customs Broker & Attorney Partnerships
**Referral Fee:** $500 per successful removal referral
**White-label API:** $2,000/month + 30% revenue share
**Target:** Top 500 customs brokers, 200 trade attorneys
**Features:**
- White-label alert monitoring dashboard
- API integration into broker/attorney workflow
- Co-branded client communications
- Revenue share on referred conversions
- Marketing materials and training

## Revenue Streams

### Primary: Active Alert Removals (40% of revenue)
- **Model:** $50K flat fee per removal case
- **Volume:** 50-200 cases annually (Year 1-3)
- **Timeline:** 4-month average completion
- **Margin:** 70% (attorney review cost, FDA submission labor)

### Secondary: Crisis Insurance Activations (30% of revenue)
- **Model:** $0/month + $20K per activation
- **Frequency:** 10-20% of monitoring customers activate annually
- **Margin:** 80% (petitions automated, lower touch)

### Tertiary: Risk Monitoring Subscriptions (20% of revenue)
- **Model:** Recurring SaaS subscription
- **Churn target:** <8% monthly (high urgency if at-risk)
- **Expansion path:** OAI companies upgrade to higher tiers as risk increases

### Quaternary: Partnership Revenue (10% of revenue)
- **Model:** Referral fees + white-label subscriptions
- **Growth driver:** Brokers/attorneys bundle with existing services
- **Margin:** 90% (minimal delivery cost)

## Pricing Rationale

### Why $0/month + $20K Activation Works
1. **Psychological alignment**: "We win when you win" builds trust
2. **Risk reversal**: Importer pays nothing unless alert occurs
3. **High willingness to pay**: $20K feels cheap vs $50K-500K/month loss
4. **Urgency conversion**: When alert hits, price insensitivity peaks
5. **Preventive value**: 6-month prediction window enables proactive action

### Why $50K for Active Removal
1. **Market anchoring**: 50% of typical attorney fees ($50-200K)
2. **Speed premium**: 4 months vs 12-24 months = massive savings
3. **Success guarantee**: 50% refund if petition rejected
4. **Value math**: 1 month faster = $50K-500K in recovered revenue
5. **Competitive differentiation**: AI-powered vs manual attorney work

### Why $799-2,499 Monitoring Tiers
1. **Value anchoring**: $800/month prevents $20K activation + $500K/month loss
2. **Tiered adoption**: Start with high-risk products, expand coverage
3. **Budget alignment**: Fits compliance department budgets
4. **Insurance framing**: "Insurance policy" vs "software subscription"

### Why $500 Referral Fee
1. **Broker motivation**: 1% of removal fee, high margin for broker
2. **Alignment**: Broker gets paid when we get paid
3. **Low friction**: Easy to justify referral to clients in crisis
4. **Scalability**: Enables broker channel growth

## Seasonal Patterns

### Q1: High Volume
- Post-holiday import surge = more FDA scrutiny
- New Import Alerts often issued after Q4 import peak
- Opportunity: OAI monitoring conversions

### Q2: Moderate Volume
- Regulatory enforcement typically consistent
- Planning for peak import season (Q4)
- Opportunity: preventive monitoring contracts

### Q3: Low Volume
- Summer lull in imports
- FDA staffing may be reduced (slower processing)
- Opportunity: back-office improvement, tech development

### Q4: High Volume
- Holiday import peak = highest FDA activity
- Urgent removal needs (holiday deadlines)
- Opportunity: active removal conversions
