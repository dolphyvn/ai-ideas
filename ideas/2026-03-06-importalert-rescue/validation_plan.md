# Validation Plan: ImportAlertRescue.ai

## Hypothesis
Companies on FDA Import Alert will pay $20K-50K for AI-powered removal because each month on alert costs $50K-500K in blocked shipments and lost revenue.

## Validation Method: Direct-to-Crisis MVP

### Week 1: Active Alert Discovery
**Action:**
- Scrape FDA Import Alert database for all 300+ active alerts
- Identify affected companies (manufacturer name, importer name, contact info)
- Prioritize: Fresh produce, seafood, high-value categories (higher urgency)
- Build database of 1,000+ companies currently on alert

**Success Metric:**
- Identify 500+ directly contactable companies
- 20+ high-value targets (>$100K monthly import volume)

### Week 2-3: Direct Outreach + Landing Page
**Action:**
- Launch simple landing page (Vercel + Next.js)
- Value prop headline: "On FDA Import Alert? Get Off in 4 Months, Not 18"
- CTA: "Get Free Removal Roadmap" + "Check Alert Status"
- Direct email to 100 companies on alert: "You're on Import Alert #XX-XX. Here's your removal path."
- Cold call top 20 high-value targets

**Success Metric:**
- 25+ responses from affected companies
- 5+ companies request full removal roadmap
- 2 companies agree to paid removal

### Week 4: Pilot Removal Case
**Action:**
- Onboard 1-2 companies into removal process
- Generate AI petition based on alert details + remediation evidence
- Submit to FDA (manual process first)
- Track: Petition acceptance, clean shipment monitoring, removal timeline

**Success Metric:**
- Petition accepted by FDA
- At least 1 company shows measurable progress toward removal
- Company willing to provide testimonial/case study
- Referral to 1+ other affected company

## Pilot Design Details

### Target Company Profile
- Currently on FDA Import Alert (verified)
- $50K+ monthly losses from blocked shipments
- Single manufacturer or single origin (simpler case)
- Has or can get facility audits, lab reports (evidence available)
- English-speaking or has translator

### Pilot Deliverables
- **Removal Roadmap**: Step-by-step path to alert removal
- **AI-Generated Petition**: FDA-compliant removal petition with evidence attachments
- **Clean Shipment Tracking**: Monitoring of next 5 shipments for clean examination
- **FDA Response Coordination**: Follow-through on FDA requests for additional information
- **Timeline Commitment**: Target 4-month removal (vs industry 12-24 months)

### Pilot Pricing
- $25,000 flat fee (50% discount for pilot)
- 50% upfront, 50% upon successful petition submission
- Success guarantee: petition rejected = 50% refund

## Pivot Options if Validation Fails

**Pivot 1: Pure Prevention (OAI Monitoring)**
- If removal cases don't convert, focus on preventive monitoring
- $799/month subscription for OAI-risk companies
- Value prop: "Never face Import Alert in the first place"
- Lower urgency but larger addressable market

**Pivot 2: Attorney-Only Strategy**
- Pivot to B2B: sell exclusively to trade attorneys
- White-label SaaS for petition generation
- Higher volume through attorney distribution
- $2,000/month per attorney

**Pivot 3: Specialization Pivot**
- Narrow to single product category (e.g., seafood from Vietnam)
- Deep expertise in specific alert types
- Higher success rate, narrower market
- Category leadership in high-value segment

**Pivot 4: DIY Petition Tool**
- Self-service petition generator ($997 one-time)
- No removal service, just petition drafting
- High volume, lower touch
- Target: smaller importers, budget-constrained

**Pivot 5: FDA Compliance Expansion**
- Expand beyond Import Alert to general FDA import compliance
- Broader market, less urgency
- Full-stack compliance platform
- Higher CAC, larger TAM

## Customer Discovery Questions

### For Active Alert Companies:
1. How long have you been on Import Alert? What's your monthly revenue loss?
2. What have you tried so far for removal? What was the outcome?
3. How much have you spent on attorneys/consultants for this issue?
4. If you could get off in 4 months for $50K, would you do it?
5. What's your biggest frustration with the current removal process?
6. Do you have the required evidence (facility audits, lab tests)?

### For OAI-Risk Companies (Not Yet on Alert):
1. Are you aware that OAI status often precedes Import Alert?
2. What would you do if your shipments were blocked tomorrow?
3. Would you pay $0/month + $20K if alert occurs, for preventive monitoring?
4. How do you currently track your FDA compliance status?
5. What's your risk tolerance for supply chain disruption?

### For Trade Attorneys:
1. How many Import Alert removal cases do you handle annually?
2. What's your typical fee? Timeline?
3. Would you white-label AI petition generation?
4. What are your biggest pain points in the removal process?
5. Would you refer clients if we offered faster, cheaper removal?

### For Customs Brokers:
1. How often do your clients get placed on Import Alert?
2. What do clients typically do when this happens?
3. Would you offer Import Alert protection as a value-add?
4. What referral fee would motivate you to recommend us?

## Legal and Regulatory Considerations

### FDA Compliance
- Petition submissions must comply with FDA guidelines
- No guarantees of removal (regulatory discretion)
- Proper disclaimers about timelines and outcomes

### Attorney Referral Compliance
- Cannot practice law without license
- Clear positioning: technology tool, not legal advice
- Attorney review requirement for final petitions

### Data Privacy
- Company import data is commercially sensitive
- Secure handling of shipment and facility information
- Compliance with data protection regulations
