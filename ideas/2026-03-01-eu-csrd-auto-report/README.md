## Concept

AI-powered platform that automates Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) compliance for EU companies. Uses LLMs to extract ESG data from financial systems, map to ESRS (European Sustainability Reporting Standards), and generate audit-ready sustainability reports in local EU languages.

## Local Credentials Required

**France**:
- **Commissaire aux Comptes** (French statutory auditor) certification
- Registration with **ACPR** (Autorité de Contrôle Prudentiel et de Résolution)
- Membership in **OECCA** (Ordre des Experts-Comptables)

**Germany**:
- **Wirtschaftsprüfer** (German public auditor) certification
- Registration with **BaFin** (Bundesanstalt für Finanzdienstleistungsaufsicht)
- Membership in **IDW** (Institut der Wirtschaftsprüfer)

**EU Level**:
- **EFRAG** (European Financial Reporting Advisory Group) approval for ESRS standards
- **ESMA** (European Securities and Markets Authority) enforcement coordination

## Why US AI Startups Won't Enter

1. **ESRS complexity**: EU-specific standards (12 cross-cutting + sector-specific), different from SASB/TCFD/GRI
2. **27 national implementations**: Each country implements ESRS differently (e.g., France requires French-language reports via specific government portal)
3. **Audit requirements**: Must partner with local statutory auditors (Commissaire aux Comptes) skeptical of US solutions
4. **Regulatory fragmentation**: French DGCCRF, German BaFin, Dutch AFM each have different enforcement
5. **Language barriers**: Reports must be in local language (French, German, Dutch, Polish)
6. **Data localization**: ESG data in local ERP systems (SAP Germany, Sage France) with different schemas
7. **US GDPR friction**: US companies struggle with GDPR-compliant data processing
