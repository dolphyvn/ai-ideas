# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**EU CSRD Auto-Report**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered platform that automates EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) compliance. Handles ESRS data collection, double materiality assessment, report generation, and statutory auditor sign-off coordination.

## Problem:
- **50,000+ EU companies** must comply with CSRD
- **Phase 1 deadlines ALREADY PASSED** - companies panicking
- **Fines up to €10M or 5% of revenue** for non-compliance
- **27 national implementations** - extreme complexity
- **€500K-€2M consulting fees** per company (manual process)

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** EU Mid-Market Companies
- CAC 40, SBF 120, MDAX, FTSE 250 (large caps already hiring consultants)
- Mid-market firms (€50M-€500M revenue) - underserved
- French and German companies (Phase 1, immediate pain)

**Secondary:** Statutory auditors (Big Four France/Germany), ESG consulting firms

## Why Now:
1. **Phase 1 deadlines PASSED**: Companies already missing deadlines, facing fines
2. **ESRS complexity**: 12 cross-cutting standards, 27 national implementations
3. **Language barriers**: French/German requirements add complexity
4. **Audit shortage**: Statutory auditors overwhelmed by CSRD workload
5. **Fine pressure**: Up to €10M or 5% of revenue (existential for mid-market)

## AI Advantage:
- **ESRS mapping**: AI maps company data to 12 ESRS standards
- **Double materiality**: Automated financial vs. impact materiality assessment
- **Data collection**: Integrates with ERP, ESG systems, sensors
- **Report generation**: Auto-creates XBRL-tagged reports in local language
- **Audit coordination**: Prepares workpapers for statutory auditor sign-off

## Viral Mechanism:
- **CAC 40 case studies**: First compliant companies share results
- **Industry associations**: Sector-specific associations spread results
- **Chambre de Commerce**: French chamber networks
- **EU conferences**: CSRD summit, ESG World Forum

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Company Subscription:**
- **Standard**: €50K/year - Single entity, basic ESRS coverage
- **Advanced**: €100K/year - Multi-entity, full ESRS, double materiality
- **Enterprise**: €200K/year - Unlimited + statutory audit coordination + local language

**Implementation:**
- €25K one-time (ERP integration, ESRS training, data migration)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: €30K (EU conferences, direct sales, partnerships)
- LTV: €225K (36-month retention, switching costs massive)
- Gross Margin: 80%

## Moat:
1. **Regulatory moat**: ESRS complexity (12 standards, 27 implementations)
2. **Language barrier**: French/German documentation requirements
3. **Statutory auditor**: French/German auditors must sign off
4. **Integration depth**: ERP systems (SAP, Oracle) + ESG platforms
5. **Data moat**: Company-specific ESRS mapping improves with use

## MVP in 16 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-4:**
- Focus: ONE ESRS standard (ESRS E1 - General Principles)
- ONE country (France - Phase 1, immediate pain)
- Manual ESRS mapping

**Weeks 5-8:**
- Build AI data extraction engine (ERP + ESG sources)
- Double materiality assessment automation
- French language report generation

**Weeks 9-12:**
- Partner with 3 French mid-market companies
- Deploy pilot for 2024 reporting (extended filing)
- Statutory auditor review + sign-off

**Weeks 13-16:**
- Add remaining ESRS standards (cross-cutting)
- Expand to Germany (Phase 2 timeline)
- XBRL tagging automation

**Validation Metrics:**
- 3+ companies use for actual CSRD filing
- Statutory auditor accepts reports
- 50%+ time reduction vs. consulting
- Payback <6 months

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: GPT-4 (French/German), Claude 4.6 (ESRS analysis)
- **Integrations**: SAP, Oracle, Microsoft Dynamics, Teamtailor
- **Standards**: ESRS database, national implementation mappings
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (Frankfurt, Paris regions)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: €250K MRR (30 companies × €8.3K ARPU)
- **Year 2**: €1.25M MRR (150 companies + enterprise)
- **Year 3**: €4M MRR (500 companies + audit firm partnerships)

## Risk Factors:
1. **Regulatory changes**: ESRS may evolve, requiring updates
2. **Competition**: Big Four consulting firms adding AI tools
3. **Political risk**: EU may delay or modify CSRD
4. **Audit liability**: Wrong report = auditor responsibility
5. **Country complexity**: 27 implementations = localization nightmare

## Competitive Threat:
- **Big Four Consulting**: KPMG, EY, Deloitte, PwC offering CSRD services
- **Sustainalytics**: ESG ratings, not CSRD compliance automation
- **Workiva**: ESG reporting, not EU-specific
- **Bloomberg**: ESG data, not compliance workflow

**Differentiation**: Purpose-built for CSRD, localized for EU countries, AI-first vs. consulting-heavy

## Exit Path:
- **Big Four France/Germany**: Acquisition for CSRD practice
- **SAP/Oracle**: Integration into ERP sustainability modules
- **ESG software providers**: Envea, Metrio, Novisto
- **EU RegTech**: Regional expansion
