## Name

**LitigationDeflectAI - Predictive Lawsuit Prevention for P&C Insurance Carriers**

---

## Core Concept

AI-powered litigation prevention platform that predicts which claimants are likely to sue, analyzes claim communication patterns for escalation signals, recommends de-escalation strategies, and quantifies settlement tradeoffs. The system trains on millions of historical claims to identify behavioral, demographic, and situational factors that correlate with lawsuit filing, enabling carriers to intervene before litigation becomes inevitable.

**The Bridge:** Reactive Lawsuit Defense ($100K+ per case) → [AI] → Preemptive Escalation Prevention (Settle before suit)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Litigation Catastrophe

- **3M+ lawsuits filed annually** from denied or disputed P&C insurance claims
- **Average litigation cost: $100K-500K** vs. $5K-25K pre-suit settlement
- **12-24 month resolution time** for lawsuits vs. 2-8 weeks for settlement
- **20% of claims generate 80% of legal costs** - but carriers can't identify which 20%
- **Attorney involvement = 10x settlement costs** - once lawyer appears, negotiation leverage evaporates

### The Current Reality

```
"We handle 500,000 claims annually. About 2% end up in litigation,
but those 10,000 claims cost us $1.2B in legal fees - more than
the other 98% combined. The problem is, by the time we know a
claim will go legal, it's too late. The attorney is already involved,
the position is entrenched, and we're looking at $200K minimum.
We'd happily pay $25K upfront to avoid $200K later, but we have
no way to identify which claims will escalate."
- VP Claims, Top 10 P&C Carrier
```

### Why This Happens

| Barrier | Impact | Frequency |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **No prediction** | Can't identify high-risk claims until too late | Always |
| **Reactive response** | Defense starts after lawsuit filed | Always |
| **Human bias** | Adjusters miss subtle escalation signals | Often |
| **Fragmented data** | Claim notes, calls, emails not analyzed together | Always |
| **Settlement uncertainty** | Don't know which claims warrant early settlement | Always |
