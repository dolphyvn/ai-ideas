## Name

**SubroRoverAI - AI-Powered Subrogation Recovery for P&C Insurance Carriers**

## Core Concept

AI-powered subrogation recovery engine that automatically identifies subrogation opportunities, calculates recoverable amounts, generates demand letters, and tracks recovery progress. The system analyzes claim patterns, police reports, contracts, and liability determinations to find recovery opportunities human adjusters miss, then automates the demand letter process and negotiates settlements.

**The Bridge:** Manual Subrogation Review (30% recovery rate) → [AI] → Automated Recovery Identification (80%+ recovery rate)

```

    ↓

    ↓

    ↓
Recovery tracking (follow-up, escalation, litigation referral)
    ↓
Recovery realized (payment received, file closed)
    ↓
Learning feedback (recovery outcomes train next-gen model)
```
