## Core Concept

AI-powered pesticide record-keeping and compliance system. The platform automatically generates required pesticide use reports (state-mandated), tracks restricted entry intervals (REI) and pre-harvest intervals (PHI), and integrates with weather data to optimize application timing. Uses public pesticide label data and state reporting requirements.

**The Bridge:** Manual Pesticide Record-Keeping (error-prone, time-intensive) → [AI] → Automated Compliance Reporting (label database, REI/PHI tracking, use reports)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Pesticide Compliance Crisis

- **CA, OR, WA mandate pesticide use reporting** for all applications
- **$250-1,000 per violation** in CA fines
- **REI violations** = worker safety penalties
- **PHI violations** = residue testing failures = rejected shipments ($10K-$100K+)
- **Pest Control Advisors (PCAs)** serve 20-50 farms each - overwhelmed

### The Current Reality

```
"We spray 30+ products across our 500 acres of almonds. Keeping
track of REI (worker re-entry) and PHI (pre-harvest interval)
for each product is a nightmare. One mistake—entering a field
too soon or harvesting too early—and we lose the entire crop.
We had a shipment rejected last year for residue issues. Cost
us $75K. We'd pay $15K annually for AI that actually tracks
this automatically."
- Almond Grower, Central Valley CA
```
