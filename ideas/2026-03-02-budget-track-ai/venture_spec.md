# BudgetTrackAI - AI-Powered Construction Budget Forecasting & Overrun Prediction

**Date:** 2026-03-02
**Status:** GO - 8.1/10

## Core Concept

AI-powered construction budget forecasting platform that analyzes project data, predicts cost overruns, identifies risk factors, and generates proactive alerts. The system learns from historical project performance, monitors real-time spending patterns, and provides actionable recommendations to keep projects on budget.

**The Bridge:** Reactive Budget Management (surprised by overruns) → [AI] → Predictive Budget Control (early warnings, actionable insights)

---

## Problem Statement

### The Budget Overrun Crisis

- **98% of major construction projects** experience cost overruns
- **Average overrun: 28%** above budget
- **$2T+ annually** wasted on construction overruns globally
- **No early warning** - overruns discovered too late to correct
- **Reactive management** - decisions made after money is lost

### The Current Reality

```
"We had a $25M budget. By the time we realized we were
$4M over, it was too late. Change orders had stacked
up, subcontractor costs had spiraled, and we were
too far down the road to recover. We absorbed the loss
and ate our margin. If we'd had a 3-month warning,
we could have scope-crippled and saved half of it.
We'd pay $75K annually for predictive budget intelligence."
- VP of Operations, Regional GC
```
