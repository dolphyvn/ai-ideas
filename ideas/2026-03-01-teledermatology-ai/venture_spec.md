# AI Venture Spec

## Name:
**TeleDermatologyAI - Teledermatology Platform**

## Core Concept:
AI-powered teledermatology platform that uses computer vision to analyze skin images, triage skin conditions, refer to dermatologists, and automate prior authorization for dermatology treatments.

## Problem:
- **10,000+ dermatologists** in US (shortage = long wait times)
- **9,500 skin cancer diagnoses/day** in US (early detection = life-saving)
- **Dermatologist shortage**: 4-6 week wait times for appointments
- **Skin concerns = MASSIVE**: 1M+ patients with skin concerns (acne, eczema, psoriasis, suspicious moles)
- **Prior auth burden**: Biologics (Humira, Cosentyx) for psoriasis, eczema require prior auth
- **Image-based diagnosis**: Dermatology = visual specialty (skin images = diagnostic)

## Target Vertical:
**Primary:** Dermatology Practices
- General dermatologists
- Mohs surgeons (skin cancer)
- Pediatric dermatologists
- Teledermatology platforms

**Secondary:** Primary care physicians (dermatology referral), urgent care, patients (direct-to-consumer)

## Why Now:
1. **Skin cancer epidemic**: Melanoma = 5-year survival 99% (early) vs. 32% (late)
2. **AI computer vision**: Skin image AI reaching dermatologist-level accuracy
3. **Teledermatology boom**: COVID accelerated, patients expect virtual care
4. **Prior auth explosion**: Biologics for psoriasis, eczema = prior auth burden
5. **Smartphone cameras**: High-quality photos = DIY skin screening

## AI Advantage:
- **Skin image AI**: Computer vision (CNN) classifies skin lesions (benign vs. malignant)
- **Triage engine**: AI prioritizes urgent cases (melanoma, squamous cell carcinoma)
- **Dermatology referral**: AI matches patient to dermatologist (location, insurance, specialty)
- **Prior auth automation**: AI completes biologic prior auth (psoriasis, eczema)
- **Condition tracking**: AI tracks skin condition progression (acne, eczema, psoriasis)
- **Patient education**: AI generates treatment explanations, skincare routines

## Viral Mechanism:
- **Social media**: Instagram, TikTok (skincare = MASSIVE)
- **Patient virality**: "AI detected my skin cancer" = VIRAL
- **Dermatology conferences**: AAD (American Academy of Dermatology) annual meeting
- **Patient advocacy**: Skin Cancer Foundation, psoriasis foundations
- **Case studies**: "Caught melanoma early using AI, saved my life"

## Revenue Model:
**Per-Practice Subscription:**
- **Starter**: $1,000/month - 1-3 dermatologists, image AI + triage
- **Practice**: $3,000/month - 4+ dermatologists, unlimited + prior auth + referral network
- **Enterprise**: $8,000/month - Multi-location + white-label + DTC patient app + API

**Per-Patient add-on:**
- $5 per AI skin analysis (beyond included tiers)

**Implementation:**
- $5,000 one-time (image AI training, referral network setup, training)

**Unit Economics:**
- CAC: $150 (AAD conferences, dermatology partnerships, social media)
- LTV: $64,800 (36-month retention - switching costs high)
- Gross Margin: 85%

## Moat:
1. **Skin image AI**: Computer vision model trained on dermatology images (ISIC dataset)
2. **Dermatology expertise**: Skin conditions, biologics, prior auth criteria
3. **Referral network**: Dermatologists by location, insurance, specialty (Mohs, pediatric)
4. **Prior auth complexity**: Biologic prior auth (psoriasis, eczema, atopic dermatitis)
5. **Patient data**: Skin images + outcomes = improving AI model

## MVP in 12 Weeks:
**Weeks 1-3:**
- Focus: Skin image AI only
- ONE condition (suspicious moles - melanoma)
- One dermatology practice pilot

**Weeks 4-6:**
- Build AI skin image classifier (melanoma vs. benign)
- Triage engine (urgent vs. routine)
- Dermatology referral matching

**Weeks 7-9:**
- Partner with 5 dermatology practices
- Deploy pilot for skin image AI + triage
- Validate: AI accuracy, referral conversion

**Weeks 10-12:**
- Add 5 more conditions (acne, eczema, psoriasis, actinic keratosis, rosacea)
- Biologic prior auth module
- Prepare AAD conference demo

**Validation Metrics:**
- 90%+ skin classification accuracy (vs. dermatologist)
- 50%+ triage to correct specialist
- 3+ practices commit after pilot
- One melanoma detected using AI (early intervention)

## Tech Stack:
- **Backend**: Python/FastAPI
- **AI**: PyTorch (computer vision - CNN, ResNet), GPT-4 (prior auth completion)
- **Integrations**: Dermatology EMRs, EHR systems, imaging platforms
- **Infrastructure**: AWS (US regions, HIPAA compliant)

## Monthly Revenue Potential (Year 1–3):
- **Year 1**: $15K MRR (15 practices × $1K ARPU)
- **Year 2**: $75K MRR (75 practices + practice tier)
- **Year 3**: $225K MRR (225 practices + enterprise)

## Risk Factors:
1. **AI liability**: Wrong melanoma diagnosis = delayed treatment = liability
2. **FDA regulation**: AI medical devices may require FDA clearance
3. **Dermatologist resistance**: May resist AI encroachment
4. **Image quality**: Patient smartphone photos = variable quality
5. **Competition**: Existing teledermatology platforms (Direct Derm, SkyMD)

## Competitive Threat:
- **Direct Derm**: Teledermatology (not AI-focused)
- **SkyMD**: Teledermatology app (not AI image analysis)
- **SkinVision**: Consumer skin cancer app (not practice-focused)
- **Dermatology EMRs**: Modernizing Medicine, EMA (not AI image analysis)

**Differentiation**: Only AI platform for dermatology practices with skin image AI (computer vision), triage, and prior auth automation.
